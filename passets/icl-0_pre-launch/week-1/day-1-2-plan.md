## 💡 Pro Tips for These Two Days

1. **Front-load critical path**: C-Corp → EIN → BOI is fastest path to unblock everything else. Do this first.

2. **24-hour C-Corp filing is worth it**: $100 extra ($189 vs $89) saves 1-2 days. Time is more valuable than money here.

3. **Parallel work saves time**: Formulation (4 hrs) can happen same day as C-Corp filing (3 hrs) because they're independent tracks.

4. **Lawyer is potential bottleneck**: Check in Day 2 afternoon. If delayed, escalate immediately (don't wait until Day 4 to discover they're behind).

5. **Formulation lock is non-negotiable**: This MUST happen by Day 5. If formulator pushes back, escalate. Production delays cascade fast.

6. **83(b) calendar alert is CRITICAL**: Missing this deadline costs $10K-50K+ in taxes. Set the alert the MOMENT stock is issued.

7. **Update status.md daily**: Takes 10 min EOD, saves hours of "where were we?" confusion later.

8. **Shopify is just foundation**: Don't get sucked into designing pages. That's Week 3-4. Just pixels + theme = done.
