### IMP-M2-005: Issue Founder Stock & File 83(b) Elections
**Source**: M2: Entity & Legal (M2-04-Entity-Formation-Guide)
**Owner Role**: CoS / Founder
**Owner Name**: [TBD]
**Due**: Week 1, Day 5 (issue stock), Day 35 (83(b) deadline)
**Dependencies**: IMP-M2-004 (organizational meeting held)
**Status**: Not Started

**Action Checklist**:
- [ ] Execute Restricted Stock Purchase Agreements (RSPAs) for each founder
  - Purchase price: $0.0001/share (par value)
  - Vesting: 4-year monthly, 1-year cliff (standard)
  - Include: IP assignment, repurchase right on unvested shares
- [ ] Founders pay nominal purchase price (e.g., $850 for 8.5M shares)
- [ ] Each founder files IRS 83(b) election within 30 days
  - File with IRS (certified mail, return receipt)
  - Copy to company records
  - Copy with founder's personal tax records

**Cost**: ~$850 total (par value payment) + ~$20 (certified mail)

**CRITICAL**: 83(b) election MUST be filed within 30 days. Missing this deadline costs tens of thousands in taxes later.
