## Supplier Requirements (Week 1 Lock Checklist)

**Must Verify**:
1. **Source depth**: Deep ocean water (>600m preferred for purity)
2. **Processing**:
   - Microfiltration: 0.22µm (removes bacteria, viruses, microplastics)
   - Cold sterilization (preserves ionic state)
   - No synthetic additives
3. **Heavy metal testing** (FDA limits):
   - Lead: <0.5 ppm
   - Mercury: <0.1 ppm
   - Cadmium: <0.5 ppm
   - Arsenic: <0.2 ppm
4. **GMP certification**: Good Manufacturing Practice
5. **Certificate of Analysis (CoA)**: Batch-specific mineral profile + heavy metals + microbial

## Common Questions

**Q: Why 1000mg sodium? Isn't that high?**
A: Target population (keto/fasting/performance) has 2-3g/day supplemental sodium need. Standard diet provides 3-4g, but low-carb increases renal excretion. 1g per serving = 2-3 servings/day.

**Q: Why ocean-sourced vs synthetic minerals?**
A: Natural ionic forms (pre-chelated by phytoplankton) = higher bioavailability (66-90%+) vs synthetic salts (30-50%). Plus full trace mineral spectrum (78 vs 3-4).

**Q: Can we claim "78 minerals"?**
A: YES for ingredient claim (ocean water contains ~78 elements). CAREFUL for health claims (must substantiate benefit for each). Strategy: Lead with core electrolytes (substantiated), mention trace minerals as differentiator (no specific health claims).

**Q: What if supplier can't provide exact trace mineral amounts?**
A: Normal. Ocean water composition varies by depth/location. Focus: Verify heavy metals BELOW FDA limits. Exact trace amounts = "present at natural ocean concentrations" (sufficient for label).

**Q: How do we handle formulation changes after Week 1?**
A: DON'T. Changes delay supplier sourcing (Week 2-4) → delay first production (Week 6-8) → delay launch. Get it right in Week 1.

## Red Flags (What to Watch For)

🚩 **Supplier can't provide CoA** → Find different supplier
🚩 **Heavy metals ABOVE FDA limits** → Non-negotiable dealbreaker
🚩 **Source water <600m depth** → Risk of surface pollutants (microplastics, etc.)
🚩 **Heat sterilization** → Degrades ionic mineral structure
🚩 **Synthetic additives** → Violates "natural" positioning
🚩 **No GMP certification** → Regulatory risk
