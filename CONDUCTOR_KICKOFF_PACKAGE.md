## 1. Your Mission

You are the operations backbone of the Studio Phase. You source flash team members, manage the candidate pipeline, and handle logistics. You are NOT an admin — you are a studio coordinator who keeps the talent flowing.

**Your win condition:** Every cluster has a PM co-founder and expert reviewer on schedule. Zero gaps in the sprint calendar. The production machine never stalls waiting for people.

**The chain:** Librarian sets up the system → You fill it with people → MC onboards them on camera → They sprint → Repeat.

### What you own

- Source flash team members from platforms, communities, and referrals
- Manage the candidate pipeline for ALL flash roles (PM co-founders, Expert Reviewers, Funnel Builder, Series Producer, Product Person)
- Post on Upwork, LinkedIn, AngelList, domain-specific communities
- Screen incoming applicants (first filter)
- Coordinate with MC for entrance/exit conversations
- Schedule sprints so clusters don't bottleneck
- Track the sourcing pipeline daily

### What you don't touch

- Folder preparation or Trade maintenance (that's the Librarian)
- On-camera conversations (that's the MC)
- TUP content or grading (that's PM co-founders and Expert Reviewers)
- Final hiring decisions (that's Nilo)
- Strategy, thesis, or brand decisions (that's Nilo)

## 2. The Sourcing Playbook

You run three channels feeding into one funnel:

**Application → MC Conversation → PM/Nilo Review → Onboarding**

### Channel 1: Platforms (Primary — Week 1 focus)

| Platform | Best For | How to Post |
|----------|----------|-------------|
| **LinkedIn** | PM co-founders, CoS candidates | Job post + direct outreach to relevant profiles |
| **Upwork** | Admin roles, product person, specialists | Job listing with clear scope and timeline |
| **AngelList/Wellfound** | Operators, PM co-founders | Startup role posting with equity highlight |
| **Dribbble/Behance** | Product person, designers | Portfolio-based outreach |
| **Acting/film boards** | MC candidates | Creative role posting, emphasize on-camera work |
| **Domain subreddits** | Expert reviewers | Community posts in relevant professional subs |
| **D2C communities** | Operator, supply chain experts | Targeted posts in Slack groups, Discord servers |

**Volume:** High applicant count, heavy screening needed.
**Quality:** Variable — screening is critical.
**Cost:** Most platforms $0. LinkedIn Premium $60/mo (optional).

### Channel 2: Cold Email (Week 2+)

| Element | Detail |
|---------|--------|
| **Tool** | ListKit or similar ($100-200/mo) + email infrastructure ($50/mo) |
| **Volume** | 50-100 emails/week per role |
| **Response rate** | Expect 5-15% |
| **Process** | You send. Nilo writes templates (once per role). Claude personalizes. |
| **Quality** | Medium — good targeting = good quality. Needs tight ICP definition. |

### Channel 3: Pass-the-Bottom (Ongoing, passive)

Every person already in the system tells their network. MC mentions open roles in daily updates. Cast page has "Apply" slots. This compounds — each person brings 1-3 referrals. Quality is highest because referrals are pre-validated.

**Your job:** Make sure the referral path is easy. Ask every exiting flash team member: "Who else should we talk to?"

Use these as starting points. Customize per cluster/domain.

**Title:** PM Co-Founder — [Cluster Name] (IonWave)

**About:** IonWave is a D2C electrolyte brand being built using a venture studio model with a 900-tab operating system. We need PM co-founders to produce 4-8 TUPs (Trade Unit Projects) in specific business clusters over 2-3 week sprints.

**What you'll do:**
- Produce 4-8 TUPs for the [Cluster Name] cluster
- Work from a detailed P-tab (project plan) that defines scope and "done"
- Use Claude (AI) as a co-pilot for research and drafting
- Submit work to the Librarian for master Trade integration

**What you receive:**
- Complete project plans (P-tabs) defining every deliverable

- Claude AI as co-pilot

- PM, strategy, or business operations experience in the relevant domain
- Can work autonomously for 2-3 weeks from documented instructions
- Comfortable being recorded (MC conversations are content)
- Available 15-25 hrs/week for 2-3 weeks

**Compensation:** 0.25-0.5% equity in IonWave (vesting). Portfolio rights to all work produced.

**Career booster:** "I was a co-founding PM for [Cluster Name] in a 103-model venture studio operating system."

## 7. Escalation Guide

> *This section is role-invariant — identical framework across all Studio Phase roles.*

### Green — Handle autonomously
- Post jobs on any platform from the approved list
- Screen applicants against the checklists
- Schedule MC conversations
- Send outreach emails using approved templates
- Update the pipeline tracker
- Send daily updates to Nilo
- Ask exiting flash members for referrals

### Yellow — Ask Nilo first
- Advance a borderline candidate (scored below threshold on any critical dimension)
- A candidate asks about specific equity mechanics or vesting terms
- You want to try a sourcing channel not in the approved list
- A cluster has zero qualified candidates after 2 weeks of sourcing
- You want to modify a job description beyond filling in role/cluster specifics

### Red — Flag immediately (same day)
- Zero qualified candidates for a high-priority role after 2 weeks
- A candidate reveals a disqualifying issue after being advanced
- A flash team member drops out mid-sprint
- Any external press, legal, or sensitive inquiry about IonWave
- You suspect a candidate is misrepresenting their experience
- Equity or compensation questions you can't answer from the templates

### Escalation format (Slack DM to Nilo)

> **ESCALATION:**
> - **What:** [Brief description]
> - **Why:** [Why you can't handle it with the tools you have]
> - **My recommendation:** [What you think should happen]
> - **Urgency:** [Same day / 24 hrs / When convenient]

## 8. Handoff Checklist

> *This section is role-invariant — identical framework across all Studio Phase roles.*

### If you're being replaced mid-cycle

- [ ] **Pipeline tracker** — current, all statuses accurate, all notes complete
- [ ] **Platform logins** — document all accounts (Upwork, LinkedIn, AngelList, email tools)
- [ ] **Active outreach** — list all candidates mid-conversation, where they are in the funnel
- [ ] **Sourcing calendar** — which roles/clusters still need people and by when
- [ ] **Template library** — all JDs, cold emails, screening checklists you've been using
- [ ] **Referral contacts** — people who said "not now but maybe later" or offered referrals
- [ ] **Your daily routine** — what you do first, second, third each day
- [ ] **Open questions** — anything you've been unsure about

### End-of-cycle handoff (when all clusters are staffed)

- [ ] **Full pipeline history** — total candidates sourced, by channel, by role
- [ ] **Conversion metrics** — sourced → screened → MC conversation → approved → completed sprint
- [ ] **Best channels** — which platforms/methods produced the best candidates
- [ ] **Lessons learned** — what worked, what didn't
- [ ] **Referral network** — everyone who expressed interest but wasn't placed
