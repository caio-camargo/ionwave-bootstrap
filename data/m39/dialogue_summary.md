**Collective Upgrade**:
Added "Theory-Reality Conflict Protocol" in theoretical_foundations.md:

**When Theory and Reality Conflict**:
1. **Test theory fairly** (3-6 months, measure outcomes rigorously)
2. **Check application** (did we apply theory correctly? or misunderstand it?)
3. **Assess feedback quality** (is market feedback signal or noise? large sample or anecdote?)
4. **If theory fails after fair test** → Discard or modify theory (reality wins)
5. **Document failure** (what didn't work? why? what did we learn?)
6. **Extract meta-lesson** (theory failure is valuable data—reveals boundary conditions)

**Stance**: Theory is starting hypothesis, not final answer. Empiricism > rationalism. But give theory fair test before discarding.
