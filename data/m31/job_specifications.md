## PHASE 1 ROLES ($0-$50K MRR, Pre-PMF)

### 1. Operator (Equity-Based, Full Ownership)

**When to Hire**: Pre-launch or Month 1 if founder not operating full-time

**Role Summary**:
Operates the business end-to-end. Owns everything: supplier coordination, ads, landing pages, email, fulfillment, investor relations. Studio provides oversight, not execution.

**Key Responsibilities**:
1. Product & Supply Chain — Supplier management, inventory, quality control
2. Marketing & Growth — Meta ads, creative strategy, LP optimization, email flows
3. Operations — Fulfillment, customer service, 3PL coordination
4. Analytics & Reporting — Daily metrics, weekly reports, monthly business reviews
5. Fundraising Support — Investor updates, deck maintenance (Studio leads fundraising)

**Requirements**:
**Must Have**:
- 2+ years D2C/ecommerce experience
- Proven ability to run profitable Meta ads (CAC <$50)
- Shopify + Klaviyo + Meta Ads Manager proficiency
- Ownership mindset ("treats it like their business")
- Self-directed learner (can figure things out without constant direction)

**Nice to Have**:
- Supplement/health brand experience
- Scaled brand to $50K+ MRR
- Raised capital or supported fundraising
- Technical skills (basic HTML/CSS, Liquid templates)

**Compensation**:
- Equity: 10-25% (negotiated based on experience, timing, cash vs equity trade-off)
- Cash: Below-market or $0 early (operator accepts equity risk)
- Vesting: 4-year with 1-year cliff
- Hours: 50-60/week

**Reports To**: Studio 4 / Founder

### 2. Creative PM (Part-Time, 20-30 hrs/week)

**When to Hire**: Month 2-4, when ad creative volume >40 hrs/month

**Role Summary**:
Owns ad creation, video editing, and creative testing. On-call for creative sprints.

**Key Responsibilities**:
1. Ad Creative Production — Static ads, video ads, UGC coordination
2. Video Editing — Edit raw footage into :15-:60 ads
3. Creative Testing — Test 5-10 angles per month, identify winners
4. Design Support — Landing page assets, email headers, social graphics

**Requirements**:
**Must Have**:
- D2C ad creative portfolio (show 10+ examples)
- Video editing proficiency (Premiere, Final Cut, or CapCut)
- Understanding of Meta ad best practices (hooks, CTAs, mobile-first)
- Fast turnaround (24-48 hrs for edits)

**Nice to Have**:
- Health/supplement creative experience
- UGC direction experience
- Motion graphics skills (After Effects)

**Compensation**:
- Contractor: $25-40/hr or per-project ($50-150 per ad depending on complexity)
- No equity (contractor model)

**Reports To**: Operator

[Confidence: B | Source: Danilo tabs 640-641, 643 | IonWave: Likely Month 3-4 hire]

### 3. Ops PM / VA (Part-Time, 10-20 hrs/week)

**When to Hire**: Month 3-5, when CS >15 hrs/week or 3PL complexity increases

**Role Summary**:
Handles customer service, inventory coordination, influencer outreach execution, data entry.

**Key Responsibilities**:
1. Customer Service — Tier 1-2 support (macros, refunds, order issues)
2. Inventory Management — Restock tracking, supplier coordination
3. 3PL Coordination — Shipment monitoring, fulfillment issue resolution
4. Influencer Outreach — Templated DMs, tracking responses
5. Data Entry & Research — Competitor tracking, review mining

**Requirements**:
**Must Have**:
- Strong written communication (CS macros, professional emails)
- Detail-oriented (inventory tracking, data accuracy)
- Ecommerce platform experience (Shopify, Gorgias, or similar)
- Proactive (flags issues before they escalate)

**Nice to Have**:
- D2C/supplement brand experience
- Bilingual (English + Spanish for broader CS coverage)

**Compensation**:
- VA tier: $10-20/hr
- Experienced ops: $25-40/hr
- No equity (contractor model)

**Reports To**: Operator

[Confidence: B | Source: Danilo tabs 640-643 | IonWave: Month 4-6 hire]

## PHASE 2 ROLES ($50K-$100K MRR, Early PMF)

### 4. Growth PM (Part-Time, 20-30 hrs/week)

**When to Hire**: Month 6-12, when expanding to new channels

**Role Summary**:
Owns new channel testing and launch (Amazon, B2B, retail pilots, new acquisition channels).

**Key Responsibilities**:
1. Channel Expansion — Amazon setup, B2B outreach, retail partnerships
2. Growth Experiments — Test new acquisition channels (TikTok, Pinterest, YouTube)
3. Partnership Development — Influencer partnerships, affiliate programs
4. Market Research — Expansion markets, product line extensions

**Requirements**:
**Must Have**:
- D2C multi-channel experience (not just Meta ads)
- Proven track record launching new channels profitably
- Analytical mindset (can model channel economics before launch)
- Entrepreneurial (comfortable with ambiguity)

**Nice to Have**:
- Amazon FBA/FBM experience
- B2B/wholesale experience
- Retail buyer relationships

**Compensation**:
- Contractor: $25-40/hr + performance bonus (e.g., 5-10% of new channel revenue Month 1-3)
- Or: $50-80K part-time + equity (0.5-1%)

**Reports To**: Operator

[Confidence: B | Source: Danilo tabs 641-642 | IonWave: Phase 2 (Month 6-12), not Year 1-2]

### 5. Product Manager (Tech PM, not D2C PM)

**When to Hire**: Only if building custom tech beyond Shopify

**Role Summary**:
Traditional tech PM. Manages product roadmap, coordinates with engineering, ships features.

**When NOT to Hire**:
- If Shopify + apps meet all needs (most D2C brands)
- If no custom tools/apps being built
- If product = physical goods only (not software)

**Key Responsibilities** (if applicable):
1. Product Strategy — Roadmap, prioritization, vision
2. Customer Research — Interviews, feedback synthesis
3. Requirements — PRDs, specs, acceptance criteria
4. Coordination — Work with engineering, design, marketing
5. Launch — Go-to-market coordination
6. Analytics — Usage metrics, A/B tests, optimization
7. Backlog — Manage and prioritize feature backlog

**Requirements**:
**Must Have**:
- 3+ years product management experience
- Experience shipping products (not just managing)
- Strong analytical skills
- Customer empathy and research skills
- Clear written and verbal communication

**Nice to Have**:
- D2C/ecommerce experience
- Technical background
- Design sensibility

**Compensation**:
- Contractor: $75-150/hr
- Full-time: $100-180K + equity (0.5-1.5%)

**Reports To**: CEO or CTO

[Confidence: A | Source: Danilo tab 663 | IonWave: NOT Year 1-2 relevant — no custom tech planned]

## PHASE 3 ROLES ($100K+ MRR, Post-PMF Scale)

**Note**: All Phase 3 roles are post-PMF, typically >$1M ARR. Not relevant for IonWave Year 1-2.

### 6. CEO (Full-Time, Strategic Leadership)

**When to Hire**: Post-PMF, >$1M ARR, need external fundraising capability

**Operator vs CEO Distinction**:
| Dimension | Operator | CEO |
|-----------|----------|-----|
| Focus | Execution-focused | Strategy + execution |
| Dedication | Part of portfolio (Studio 4 model) | Full-time single venture |
| Authority | Tactical decisions | Strategic authority, hire/fire |
| Fundraising | Supports (Studio leads) | Can raise capital independently |
| Compensation | Equity-heavy (10-25%) | Equity (10-30%) + at/below-market cash |

**When NOT to Hire CEO**:
- Revenue <$1M ARR (operator model sufficient)
- Haven't achieved PMF
- Can't afford at-market comp + equity
- Complexity doesn't require full-time strategic leadership

**Key Responsibilities**:
1. P&L Ownership — Revenue, profitability, unit economics
2. Strategic Planning — Vision, roadmap, growth strategy
3. Fundraising — Lead Series A/B rounds, investor relations
4. Team Building — Hire/fire authority, culture, org design
5. Board Management — Quarterly board meetings, governance
6. External Relations — Partnerships, PR, industry positioning

**Requirements**:
**Must Have**:
- 5+ years D2C/ecommerce leadership experience
- Proven track record scaling brand to $5M+ revenue
- Fundraising experience (raised $500K+ from VCs/angels)
- P&L ownership experience
- Team management (managed 5+ people)

**Nice to Have**:
- Supplement/health brand experience
- IPO or exit experience
- Technical or finance background

**Compensation**:
- Cash: $120-200K (below market for VC-backed, at market for bootstrapped)
- Equity: 10-30% (negotiated based on stage, prior equity grants)
- Vesting: 4-year with 1-year cliff

**Reports To**: Board of Directors

[Confidence: A | Source: Danilo tabs 658-661 (CEO specs, 112 rows total) | IonWave: Phase 3+, NOT Year 1-2]

### 7. CFO (Full-Time or Fractional)

**When to Hire**: $1M+ ARR, or pre-Series A fundraising, or audit/compliance needs

**Role Summary**:
Owns financial operations, reporting, compliance, fundraising support.

**Key Responsibilities**:
1. Financial Operations — Bookkeeping, payroll, AP/AR
2. Financial Planning — Budgets, forecasts, scenario modeling
3. Fundraising Support — Pitch deck financials, investor diligence, cap table management
4. Compliance — Tax filings, audit preparation, legal/regulatory
5. Unit Economics — CAC, LTV, contribution margin, cohort analysis
6. Reporting — Monthly financials, board decks, investor updates

**Requirements**:
**Must Have**:
- CPA or MBA (finance)
- 5+ years finance experience (3+ in startups/D2C)
- Fundraising support experience (Series A/B diligence)
- Financial modeling proficiency
- Accounting software expertise (QuickBooks, Xero, or NetSuite)

**Nice to Have**:
- Big 4 accounting background
- D2C/ecommerce-specific experience
- Technical skills (SQL, Python for financial analysis)

**Compensation**:
- Fractional CFO: $150-250/hr (10-20 hrs/month early, 20-40 hrs/month at scale)
- Full-time CFO: $120-180K + equity (1-3%)

**Reports To**: CEO

[Confidence: B | Source: Danilo tab 662 (32 rows) | IonWave: Phase 3+, NOT Year 1-2]

### 8. Community Manager (CM)

**When to Hire**: Building community-driven brand, >$50K MRR, active customer engagement

**Role Summary**:
Manages community platform (email, Skool, Circle, Discord), UGC coordination, customer engagement.

**Key Responsibilities**:
1. Community Management — Moderate forums, respond to posts, facilitate discussions
2. UGC Coordination — Source customer stories, testimonials, before/afters
3. Engagement Programs — Challenges, events, AMAs, ambassador program
4. Content Creation — Weekly newsletter, community spotlights, educational content
5. Customer Feedback — Surface insights to product/ops teams

**Requirements**:
**Must Have**:
- 2+ years community management experience
- Strong written communication
- Customer empathy
- Platform expertise (Skool, Circle, Discord, or similar)

**Nice to Have**:
- D2C/supplement brand experience
- Content creation skills (video, graphics)
- UGC production experience

**Compensation**:
- Part-time: $25-40/hr (10-20 hrs/week)
- Full-time: $50-80K + equity (0.25-1%)

**Reports To**: Operator or Head of Marketing

[Confidence: B | Source: Danilo tabs 664-665 (CM specs, 68 rows) | IonWave: Phase 2-3, evaluate at $50K+ MRR if community-first strategy]

### 9. Venture Partner (Deal Maker, Equity-Only)

**When to Hire**: Phase 3, need distribution deals, partnerships, or retail introductions

**Role Summary**:
The Venture Partner is a DEAL MAKER. They don't operate ventures. They don't manage compliance. They CREATE VALUE through relationships and deals.

**Primary Function**: Make things happen that wouldn't happen otherwise.

**Key Responsibilities**:
1. Partnership Development — Identify partners (brands, influencers, retailers), negotiate terms, close deals
2. Distribution Deals — Retail introductions (Whole Foods, Sprouts), wholesale/B2B opportunities
3. Strategic Intros — Investors, advisors, domain experts
4. Deal Sourcing — New venture opportunities for Studio 4 portfolio

**Requirements**:
**Must Have**:
- Proven track record closing deals (3+ significant partnerships or distribution deals)
- Extensive network in D2C/CPG/retail
- Sales/BD background
- Relationship-building skills

**Nice to Have**:
- Supplement/health industry network
- Retail buyer relationships (Whole Foods, Target, etc.)
- Investor network

**Compensation**:
- Equity-only: 2-5% (vesting over 4 years)
- Or: Deal-based commission (5-10% of first-year deal value) + smaller equity (0.5-1%)

**Reports To**: CEO or Studio 4

[Confidence: B | Source: Danilo tabs 666-668 (VP specs, 99 rows) | IonWave: Phase 3+, NOT Year 1-2 — defer until $100K+ MRR or retail-ready]

### 10. Compliance Manager (CM, Studio 4 Role)

**When to Hire**: Pre-launch (Studio 4 provides this role)

**Role Summary**:
Daily check-ins, gate assessments, accountability, quality control. Ensures operator follows protocols, gates are met, quality standards maintained.

**Key Responsibilities**:
1. Daily Check-Ins — 15-min standups with operator
2. Gate Assessments — Phase 1 → Phase 2 transition criteria evaluation
3. Accountability — Track operator PM Competence Score, ensure >60 before ad spend
4. Quality Control — Review ad creative, LP copy, email flows for compliance
5. Studio Liaison — Escalate blockers to Studio 4 leadership

**Requirements**:
**Must Have**:
- Understanding of Studio 4 protocols (M31, M35, M39)
- D2C operational experience
- Strong process orientation
- Can give feedback without micromanaging

**Compensation**:
- $500/mo (Studio 4 budget)
- 5-10 hrs/week

**Reports To**: Studio 4 / Nilo

[Confidence: A | Source: Danilo tab 640 | IonWave: Provided by Studio 4, not a hire]

### 11. Head of Ops

**When to Hire**: $50-100K MRR, need systems design + execution capability

**Role Summary**:
Can design systems AND execute. Owns fulfillment, inventory, CS, 3PL relationships.

**Background**: Ex-consultant + operator, or scaled D2C brand

**Superpower**: Builds playbooks, then runs them

**Experience**: Built operational playbooks, managed fulfillment at scale (1K+ orders/month)

**Compensation**: $80-120K base + equity (0.5-2%)

**Reports To**: CEO or Operator

## IonWave Year 1-2 Hiring Roadmap

| Month | Revenue Target | Hire | Rationale |
|-------|---------------|------|-----------|
| Pre-launch | $0 | Operator | Founder not full-time, need execution owner |
| Month 1-2 | $5-15K MRR | None | Operator solo, building competence |
| Month 3-4 | $15-30K MRR | Creative PM (PT) | Ad volume >40 hrs/month |
| Month 5-6 | $30-50K MRR | Ops PM/VA (PT) | CS >15 hrs/week |
| Month 7-12 | $50-100K MRR | Evaluate: Growth PM or Head of Ops | Depends on constraint (new channels vs ops complexity) |
