## Key Innovations

### 1. PM Competence Framework (Novel)
**What**: 18-question scorecard assessing operator knowledge of Product (6), Market (6), Customer (6)
**Why**: "Don't spend $1 on ads until PM Score >60" — prevents $5-10K waste on incompetent ad spend
**How**: Operator must: use product 2+ weeks, do 10+ customer interviews, study 5 competitors, score >60 before ad budget

**Grade**: A (Danilo tab 669, 59 rows, complete framework with examples)
**Reusability**: High (adapt 18 questions to any product category)

---

### 2. Operator Equity Model (10-25% vs Employee 0.5-2%)
**What**: Operator gets significant equity stake (10-25%), accepts below-market cash or $0 early
**Why**: Aligns incentives (ownership mindset), conserves cash, attracts operators not employees
**How**: Start at 15% midpoint, adjust ±5% for track record/cash investment/experience/cash needs

**Grade**: B (Danilo tab 645 + 2026 equity trends + dilution waterfall math from dialogue)
**Novel**: Explicit dilution waterfall showing what's left after operator grant (prevents over-granting)

---

### 3. 3-4 PM Maximum Constraint (Anti-Scaling)
**What**: "If you need more than 4 people, system is too complex. Simplify before scaling headcount."
**Why**: Coordination cost grows exponentially, communication overhead drowns execution
**How**: 1 operator + 3 specialized PMs (Creative, Ops, Growth) running lean systems

**Grade**: A (Danilo tab 641 "THE CONSTRAINT: 3-4 PMs MAXIMUM")
**Contrarian**: Industry says "hire as needed, scale team with revenue." We say simplify systems, not add people.

---

### 4. Pipeline-Building Hiring (4-6 Weeks Early)
**What**: Post roles 4-6 weeks before hire date, build candidate pipeline proactively
**Why**: 2026 competitive market (4.5% unemployment), best candidates have multiple offers
**How**: Month 1-2 post Creative PM role (hire Month 3-4), pre-build pipeline, select from pre-vetted pool

**Grade**: B (Dialogue Round 2 upgrade + market-validated strategy)
**Impact**: 2-4 week hiring timeline achieved (vs 3-6 month scramble)

---

### 5. Hybrid Creative Sprint Model (Remote-First with Quality)
**What**: Remote 90% of time, co-locate for 3-5 day creative sprints every 6-8 weeks
**Why**: 85% of workers prefer remote (global talent pool), but creative work benefits from in-person collaboration
**How**: 4-6 sprints/year × $500-1K travel = $2-6K/year (worth it for quality + talent pool access)

**Grade**: A (2026 data: 85% remote preference, 55% prefer hybrid + Dialogue Round 6 upgrade)
**Contrarian**: Industry says "remote-optional." We say "remote-first with intentional co-location."

---

### 6. Community Engagement Sourcing Playbook
**What**: Lurk 2-4 weeks → contribute value → DM high-signal contributors (not cold-pitch)
**Why**: Higher response rate, pre-vetted by community reputation
**How**: Trends.vc, D2C Slack/Discord — observe who contributes, build credibility, personalized outreach

**Grade**: B (Dialogue Round 5 upgrade + community best practices)
**Impact**: Better sourcing quality than cold Upwork posts

## OpKit Reusability

**Target Users**: Any D2C brand (consumables, physical products, subscription)
**Adaptations**:
- **Product category**: Replace "marine electrolytes" with your product in PM Competence questions

- Service businesses (different unit economics, people = COGS)

- Supplement-specific content (adapt PM Competence questions)
