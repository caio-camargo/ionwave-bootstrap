## Dimension Deep-Dive

### 1. Evidence Coverage (8.5/10)

**Strengths**:
- **31 Danilo tabs fully processed** (640-670, 617 rows) — comprehensive source material
- **7 2026 web sources** (Upwork stats, FlexJobs remote trends, Ravio comp data, etc.) — current market context
- **5 content files** (strategy, processes, job specs, compensation, industry perspectives) — well-organized, no critical voids

**Weaknesses**:
- **IonWave-specific data C/D-grade** — All timing assumptions (Month 3-4 Creative PM hire, $15-25K MRR operator hire trigger) are pre-revenue estimates
- **No real hiring funnel data** — Candidate conversion rates, interview-to-hire ratios, sourcing channel ROI all untested

**Evidence Distribution**:
- A-grade evidence: 40% (Danilo frameworks, 2026 market research)
- B-grade evidence: 25% (Cross-TUP benchmarks, industry norms)
- C-grade evidence: 20% (Logical frameworks, untested but sound reasoning)
- D-grade evidence: 15% (IonWave timing, pre-launch assumptions)

**Score Rationale**: Excellent Danilo source coverage (A-grade), strong 2026 research (A-grade), but IonWave application layer is assumptions (C/D-grade). Balances to 8.5/10.

---

### 2. Confidence Honesty (9/10)

**Strengths**:
- **Every claim tagged with confidence grade** — No ungraded assertions
- **Explicit upgrade paths** — D-grade items have "Upgrade: Survey 3-5 operators" not "TBD"
- **Source attribution** — Every B+ grade claim has source citation with date
- **Transparent weak links** — E.g., "Operator equity 10-25% is huge range, negotiation framework TBD" (honest about ambiguity)

**Weaknesses**:
- **Minor optimism on timeline estimates** — "2-4 week hiring" may be 3-5 weeks in reality (slight optimism bias)

**Grade Defensibility**:
- A-grade: Danilo content verbatim (e.g., PM Competence Framework), 2026 research with URLs
- B-grade: Cross-TUP alignment (e.g., M19 8-11 hr onboarding), industry norms with reasoning
- C-grade: Logical frameworks (e.g., dilution waterfall math), untested but sound
- D-grade: IonWave pre-revenue assumptions, explicit about uncertainty

**Score Rationale**: Near-perfect confidence honesty. Only minor deduction for slight timeline optimism (9/10).

### 3. Upgrade Path Quality (8/10)

**Strengths**:
- **Specific upgrade paths** — E.g., "Survey 3-5 operators" (not "get market data"), "Time actual onboarding Week 1" (not "validate assumption")
- **Cost/timeline estimates** — E.g., "$0 cost, Month 1-3 timeline" for founder hrs tracking
- **Prioritized gaps** — CRITICAL vs MEDIUM vs LOW distinction

**Weaknesses**:
- **Some upgrade paths require access** — E.g., "Ask Nilo for Studio 4 equity split precedent" (not public data)
- **Validation timing assumptions** — E.g., "Month 1-3" for operator hire may slip to Month 4-5 in reality

**Example Upgrade Paths**:
| Gap | Current Grade | Target Grade | Method | Cost | Timeline |

**Score Rationale**: Upgrade paths are actionable and specific (8/10). Minor deduction for access dependencies and timing assumptions.

### 4. Actionability (8/10)

**Strengths**:
- **Decision-enabling frameworks** — PM Competence score >60 before ad spend (go/no-go gate)
- **Copy-paste templates** — Operator outreach template, interview questions, onboarding checklist
- **Phased execution plan** — IonWave hiring roadmap Month 1-12 with revenue triggers
- **Explicit trade-offs documented** — E.g., operator equity vs cash, remote vs hybrid, rigor vs speed

**Weaknesses**:
- **IonWave timing vague** — "$15-25K MRR operator hire trigger" is range, not crisp decision rule
- **Some frameworks require customization** — E.g., PM Competence 18 questions are IonWave-specific (ocean-sourced electrolytes), need adaptation for other products

**Actionability Test** (can founder execute immediately?):
- ☑ Post operator role on Upwork (yes, template provided)
- ☑ Run screening interview (yes, 5 questions listed)
- ☑ Score PM Competence (yes, 18-question scorecard with bands)

**Score Rationale**: Highly actionable (8/10). Most frameworks ready-to-use, minor deduction for IonWave timing vagueness and customization needs.

Create separate `opkit_hiring_framework.md` that extracts universal frameworks from IonWave-specific content. Current files mix the two.
