## Round Log

### ROUND 1: PM Competence Assessment Practicality
**CHALLENGE** (HR Professional): 18-question PM Competence scorecard is 2+ hour time investment. Will busy founders actually do this?

**RESPONSE** (Startup Recruiter): Test measures underlying work (10 interviews, 5 competitor studies, 2-week product usage), not just answers. Could compress to 10 critical questions.

**SYNTHESIS** (D2C Founder): In practice, assess conversationally during Focused Interview (Stage 2), not as standalone formal test.

**OUTCOME**: UPGRADED
**ACTION**: Add note to `hiring_processes.md` — PM Competence can be woven into Stage 2 interview conversationally, not separate 2-hour session. Reduces time barrier while maintaining rigor.

---

### ROUND 2: Hiring Timeline Aggressiveness
**CHALLENGE** (Startup Recruiter): 2-4 week timeline too fast in competitive 2026 market (4.5% unemployment). Best candidates have multiple offers.

**RESPONSE** (D2C Founder): Don't wait to start recruiting. Post roles 4-6 weeks before hire date. Build pipeline early, select from pre-vetted pool when ready.

**SYNTHESIS** (HR Professional): "Always-be-recruiting" mindset. Start sourcing Month 1-2 for Month 3-4 hire.

**OUTCOME**: UPGRADED
**ACTION**: Add sourcing timeline to `hiring_processes.md` and `hiring_strategy.md` IonWave roadmap — post roles 4-6 weeks early, build pipeline proactively. For IonWave: Operator role pre-launch, Creative PM Month 1-2, Ops PM Month 3.

---

### ROUND 3: Operator Equity Negotiation Framework
**CHALLENGE** (D2C Founder): 10-25% equity range is huge. What drives negotiation? How avoid over/underpaying?

**RESPONSE** (HR Professional): Anchor to market (survey 3-5 operators). Adjust for: (1) track record, (2) cash investment, (3) timing risk. Document framework transparently.

**SYNTHESIS** (Startup Recruiter): Consider dilution math. If Studio 4 = 60-65%, operator = 20%, only 15-20% left for future hires + advisors.

**OUTCOME**: UPGRADED
**ACTION**: Add dilution waterfall example to `compensation_framework.md` — show what's left after operator grant. E.g., Studio 4 65% + Operator 20% = 85% allocated, 15% remaining (10% employee pool, 3% advisors, 2% reserve).

---

### ROUND 4: Firing Timing Best Practices
**CHALLENGE** (HR Professional): Firing guide says "don't do Friday." Why Monday-Thursday better?

**RESPONSE** (D2C Founder): Friday = stew all weekend. Monday-Thursday = immediate action (job search, network, processing). More humane.

**SYNTHESIS** (Startup Recruiter): Also avoid major holidays (Thanksgiving week, Christmas) — same logic, limited support network.

**OUTCOME**: UPGRADED
**ACTION**: Minor addition to `hiring_processes.md` — firing timing: Monday-Thursday, avoid Fridays + major holiday weeks for humane reasons (network accessibility).

---

### ROUND 5: Sourcing Channel Viability (Twitter/X in 2026)
**CHALLENGE** (Startup Recruiter): Twitter engagement down post-Musk. Still viable for D2C talent sourcing?

**RESPONSE** (D2C Founder): Smaller but higher-signal. Engaged users are die-hards. Supplement with D2C communities (Trends, private Slack/Discord, EcomCrew).

**SYNTHESIS** (HR Professional): Add community engagement playbook: lurk 2-4 weeks, contribute value, then DM high-signal contributors (not cold-pitch).

**OUTCOME**: UPGRADED
**ACTION**: Expand sourcing channels in `hiring_processes.md` with community engagement tactics: lurk → contribute → DM strategy for Trends.vc, D2C Slack/Discord, EcomCrew.

---

### ROUND 6: Remote Creative Collaboration Quality
**CHALLENGE** (D2C Founder): Remote talent pool great, but creative collaboration quality suffers. How maintain quality?

**RESPONSE** (HR Professional): Hybrid model — remote 90% of time, co-location for 3-5 day creative sprints every 6-8 weeks.

**SYNTHESIS** (Startup Recruiter): Aligns with 55% preferring hybrid (FlexJobs 2026). Best of both worlds.

**OUTCOME**: UPGRADED
**ACTION**: Add hybrid/creative sprint model to `hiring_strategy.md` and `industry_perspectives.md` — remote-first with periodic in-person creative sprints (3-5 days every 6-8 weeks) for intensive creative work.
