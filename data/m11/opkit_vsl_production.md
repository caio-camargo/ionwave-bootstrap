## What This OpKit Does

Treats VSLs not as creative guesswork, but as **Trades** — with financial models, kill criteria, testing protocols, and post-mortems. Applies to any D2C brand selling via VSL (supplements, info products, SaaS, e-commerce).

**Outputs from this OpKit**:
1. VSL script with 8-section structure (Hook → Problem → Agitate → Mechanism → Proof → Offer → CTA → Objection Loop)
2. Testing protocol with statistical kill criteria
3. Financial model with cost structure and ROI scenarios
4. Production spec with FTC compliance checklist
5. Iteration system with post-mortem template

**Time to execute**: 2-4 weeks from script lock to validated winning VSL (or all killed).

**Investment**: $1,200-2,500 per VSL (production + testing). Expect 10-20% winner rate (1-2 winners from 5-10 angles tested).

## 12-Step Instructions

### PHASE 1: IMAGINATION (Week 1)

**Step 1: Research Angles**
- Study 5-10 competitor VSLs in your category
- Identify 3-5 core mechanisms (why your product works differently)
- Interview 10-20 customers: "What almost stopped you from buying? What convinced you?"
- Draft 3 angle options:
  - Angle A: [Mechanism + avatar]
  - Angle B: [Different mechanism + avatar]
  - Angle C: [Different mechanism + avatar]

**Deliverable**: 3 documented angles with hypotheses (e.g., "We believe biohackers 28-45 will respond to ocean minerals > synthetic minerals mechanism at CPA < $35")

---

**Step 2: Select Test Portfolio**
- Pick 1 angle to test first (highest conviction or lowest cost to validate)
- Create 3-5 hook variants on that angle (use Hook Library scaffold)
- Set kill criteria: CPA target, hook rate minimum, test budget max

**Deliverable**: Testing plan with budget allocation per hook variant

---

### PHASE 2: SCRIPT DEVELOPMENT (Week 1-2)

**Step 3: Write Script Using 8-Section Structure**

Use the scaffold:

1. **HOOK** (0:00-0:05): Bold claim, question, or story. Must match ad hook.
2. **PROBLEM** (0:05-0:30): Describe pain in their language. Be specific.
3. **AGITATE** (0:30-1:00): Future-pace negative outcome. Stack frustration.
4. **MECHANISM** (1:00-2:00): Explain WHY your solution works (not features).
5. **PROOF** (2:00-3:00): Testimonials, data, credentials. FTC compliant.
6. **OFFER** (3:00-3:30): What they get, price anchor, guarantee, urgency.
7. **CTA** (3:30-4:00): Clear instruction, button, final objection handle.
8. **OBJECTION LOOP** (4:00-5:00, optional): Top 3 objections addressed.

**Deliverable**: Full script (600-800 words for 3-4 min VSL)

---

**Step 4: Compliance Review (MANDATORY for Health/Supplement)**

Run script through FTC compliance checklist:
- [ ] All health claims substantiated by RCT OR use structure/function language only
- [ ] NO fictional testimonials (real customers with signed releases only)
- [ ] Comparative claims verifiable (e.g., "3 minerals vs 78" backed by ingredient labels)
- [ ] Guarantee language matches actual refund policy
- [ ] Material connections disclosed (if customers paid for testimonials)

**Deliverable**: Attorney-approved script OR sanitized version with structure/function claims

---

### PHASE 3: PRE-PRODUCTION (Week 2)

**Step 5: Assemble Production Team**
- **UGC Creator** (raw footage): $100-500 per creator, need 2-3
- **Video Editor**: $100-500 per finished VSL
- **Motion Designer** (optional): $100-300 for graphics/animations
- **Music**: $15-30/mo subscription (Epidemic Sound, Artlist)

**Budget**: $450-1,200 per VSL (mid-tier quality recommended)

---

**Step 6: Create Shot List & Gather Assets**

Shot list template:
- Hook visuals (black screen → product reveal, or B-roll montage)
- Problem B-roll (show the pain — tired people, confusion, stacks of pills)
- Mechanism explainer graphics (diagrams, comparisons, scientific visuals)
- Proof overlays (testimonial quote cards, data visualizations)
- Product hero shots (packaging, usage, benefits)
- CTA visuals (button animation, guarantee badge)

B-roll sources: Pexels (free), Artgrid ($25/mo), Storyblocks ($30/mo)

**Deliverable**: Shot list + B-roll library ready

---

### PHASE 4: PRODUCTION & POST (Week 2-3)

**Step 7: Film/Edit VSL**
- Film/record per shot list (1-2 days)
- Edit master version (3-5 days)
- Create platform variants:
  - Meta: 1:1 square or 4:5 vertical (captions MANDATORY)
  - TikTok: 9:16 vertical (fast pacing, text overlays)
  - YouTube: 16:9 horizontal (longer tolerance, 5-10 min OK)

**Deliverable**: Master VSL + 3 platform variants exported

---

### PHASE 5: TESTING (Week 3-4)

**Step 8: Launch Test Campaign**

Test parameters:
- Budget: $150-250 per variant minimum
- Impressions: 3,000-5,000 per variant target
- Duration: 5-7 days (account for day-of-week variance)
- Audience: BROAD (don't narrow targeting during creative tests)

**Platforms**: Allocate 50% Meta, 35% YouTube, 15% TikTok (adjust based on demo)

---

**Step 9: Apply Kill Criteria**

**Hard kills** (stop immediately):
- CPA > 3x target after $150 spend AND minimum 10 purchases
- Hook rate < 15% after 2,000 impressions
- Zero purchases after $250 spend

**Soft kills** (evaluate):
- CPA 2-3x target after $250 spend
- High CTR but zero conversions (landing page issue, not VSL)

**NEVER kill** based on: single-day data, gut feeling, before minimum impressions

**Deliverable**: Winner identified (CPA < 1.5x target) OR all variants killed

---

### PHASE 6: SCALING (Week 4+)

**Step 10: Scale Winner Methodically**

Scaling phases:
- **Validate**: $100-200/day for 5 days (confirm CPA stability)
- **Scale 1**: $300-500/day, increase 20% every 48 hrs if CPA holds
- **Scale 2**: $500-1000/day, duplicate to new ad sets/audiences
- **Scale 3**: $1000+/day, launch on additional platforms, create derivatives

**CPA creep tolerance**:
- < 15% increase = green light, continue scaling
- 15-30% increase = yellow, test new hooks
- > 30% increase = red, stop and diagnose

**Deliverable**: Scaled VSL running at $500-1000/day with stable CPA

---

### PHASE 7: POST-MORTEM (After Test Cycle)

**Step 11: Document Learnings**

Complete post-mortem template (see Iteration System scaffold):
- What converted (hook rate, retention curve, best section)
- What dropped off (fall-off points, creative weaknesses)
- Qualitative feedback (comments, DMs, objections)
- Lessons for next VSL (replicate, avoid, hypotheses to test)

**Deliverable**: Post-mortem doc archived, learnings transferred to next VSL

---

### PHASE 8: PORTFOLIO MANAGEMENT (Ongoing)

**Step 12: Build VSL Portfolio**

Don't bet on one VSL. VSLs fatigue after 4-6 weeks.

**Portfolio strategy**:
- Month 1: Test 3-5 angles → Expect 1 winner
- Month 2: Scale M1 winner, test 3 new angles → Expect 1 more winner
- Month 3: 2 winners scaling, test 3 new → Expect 1 more winner

**By Month 3**: 3 winning VSLs, each on its own scaling curve. Total investment: $7,500-15,000. Expected monthly revenue: $30,000-100,000.

**Deliverable**: Portfolio health dashboard tracking winner rate, avg CPA, best hook types

## Grading Rubric (Self-Assessment)

Use this rubric to grade your VSL content before launching:

| Criterion | F (Fail) | C (Acceptable) | A (Excellent) |
|-----------|----------|---------------|---------------|
| **FTC Compliance** | Fictional testimonials, unsubstantiated health claims | Some D-grade claims but documented upgrade paths | Attorney-approved, all claims substantiated OR structure/function only |
| **Script Structure** | Missing sections, unclear flow | 8 sections present, logical flow | Each section hits purpose, smooth transitions, retention > 20% |
| **Hook Strength** | Hook rate < 15% | Hook rate 20-30% | Hook rate > 30% |
| **Testing Rigor** | No kill criteria, gut-based decisions | Kill criteria set, statistical thresholds | Statistical significance, min purchase thresholds, CPA creep tolerance |
| **Production Quality** | Low-res B-roll, poor audio, distracting graphics | Mid-tier quality, clean edits | Premium footage, pro VO, polished animations |
| **Financial Modeling** | No ROI model, unclear break-even | Cost structure documented, break-even calculated | 3 scenarios (conservative/base/aggressive), platform-specific CPA targets |
| **Iteration System** | No post-mortem, learnings lost | Post-mortem template filled | Portfolio dashboard tracks meta-patterns, kill-and-pivot protocol established |

**Passing grade**: C+ or better on all 7 criteria. F on FTC Compliance = CANNOT LAUNCH.

## Adaptation Guide

**For different product types**:

| Product Type | Script Length | Mechanism Complexity | Proof Type | Expected CPA Range |
|--------------|--------------|---------------------|------------|-------------------|
| **Low-ticket supplement** ($20-50) | 3-5 min | Simple (1 key mechanism) | Testimonials + data | $20-40 |
| **Mid-ticket supplement** ($50-100) | 5-8 min | Moderate (2-3 mechanisms) | Clinical data + testimonials | $30-60 |
| **Info product** ($100-500) | 10-20 min | Complex (education required) | Authority + case studies | $50-150 |
| **SaaS** ($50-200/mo) | 5-10 min | Feature demo + ROI calc | Customer ROI stories | $100-300 |
| **High-ticket coaching** ($1K-10K) | 15-30 min | Story-driven, transformation | Before/after case studies | $200-1,000 |

**For different demographics**:

| Demographic | Platform Priority | Hook Type | Proof Type | Notes |
|-------------|------------------|-----------|------------|-------|
| **18-34** | TikTok (60%), Meta (30%) | UGC testimonial, result lead | Peer testimonials, influencer endorsements | Fast pacing, text overlays |
| **28-45** | Meta (50%), YouTube (35%) | Authority, contrarian | Data + expert credentials | Mid-length (3-5 min) optimal |
| **45-65** | YouTube (50%), Meta (40%) | Story, question | Detailed explanations, trust signals | Longer VSLs (5-10 min) tolerated |
