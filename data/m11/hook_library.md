## Hook Testing Protocol

**Process** (within a winning angle):

1. **Produce 5 hook variants** — Use library above as starting frameworks, adapt for IonWave
2. **Test each as first 5 seconds** — Same body/CTA, only hook changes
3. **Spend $20-30 per hook variant** — Run for 2-3 days
4. **Measure 3 metrics**:
   - Hook Rate (ThruPlay/Impressions)
   - Hold Rate at 0:15 (are they staying past the hook?)
   - CPA (are they buying?)
5. **Kill threshold**: Hook rate < 20% after $50 spend
6. **Winner criterion**: Highest hook rate with acceptable CPA (< 1.5x target)
7. **Lock winner** — Move to next test variable (body or offer)
8. **Repeat every 2-3 weeks** — Combat creative fatigue

**Expected improvement**: Hook optimization typically improves CPA by 20-40% vs original hook.

## Hook Design Principles

### 1. First 3 Words Matter Most

The first 3 words determine if someone stops scrolling.

**Strong openers**:
- "Your supplements..." (direct address)
- "I discovered..." (curiosity)
- "Stop drinking..." (command)

**Weak openers**:
- "In today's world..." (generic)
- "Many people..." (vague)
- "It's important..." (boring)

### 2. Match Hook to Ad Creative

If your ad hook says "Your sports drink is dehydrating you," your VSL hook MUST start with the same claim. Mismatch = instant drop-off.

**Hook consistency rule**: The first sentence of your VSL must echo the ad that drove the click.

### 3. Visual Hook Must Complement Verbal Hook

| Verbal Hook | Visual Hook (First Frame) | Why It Works |
|-------------|---------------------------|--------------|
| "Your supplements are lying to you." | Pills falling into trash | Reinforces "throw it away" message |
| "I tested every electrolyte brand..." | 10 bottles lined up on counter | Shows credibility, effort |
| "75% of Americans are dehydrated..." | Person collapsing mid-workout | Makes stat visceral, not abstract |

**Bad visual hooks**: Stock footage unrelated to claim (ocean waves for "your supplement is lying" — doesn't connect).

### 4. Avoid FTC Compliance Landmines in Hooks

**CANNOT SAY** (unsubstantiated health claims):
- "Cure your fatigue in 3 days"
- "Boost your immune system 10x"
- "Eliminate brain fog permanently"

**CAN SAY** (structure/function claims):
- "Support healthy hydration"
- "Help maintain mineral balance"
- "Promote cellular function"

**OR**: Use testimonial framing with disclaimer:
- "I stopped cramping in 3 days" [Disclaimer: Individual results vary. Not typical outcomes.]
