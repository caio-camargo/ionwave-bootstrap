## Production Checklist

- [ ] Script locked and approved
- [ ] FTC compliance review complete (MANDATORY for health claims)
- [ ] Creator agreements signed (usage rights, licensing terms)
- [ ] Shot list finalized
- [ ] B-roll sourced (stock footage or original)
- [ ] Graphics/animations brief created
- [ ] Music licensed
- [ ] Platform formats identified (Meta/TikTok/YouTube)
- [ ] Test budget allocated ($500-1000 minimum)

## B-Roll Requirements

### Must-Have Footage (Tier 1)
- Ocean waves (deep ocean, not beach)
- Marine environment (underwater, clean/pristine)
- Person drinking from sachet (natural setting, not studio)
- Workout recovery scenes (stretching, hydration, relaxation)
- Morning routine (natural light, minimalist)

### Nice-to-Have Footage (Tier 2)
- Lab/science imagery (microscope, test tubes — generic, not specific to IonWave)
- Nature/health montage (hiking, yoga, strength training)
- Comparison visuals (pills vs sachet, synthetic vs natural)

### Stock Footage Sources
- [Pexels](https://www.pexels.com) — Free (ocean, workout, morning routine)
- [Artgrid](https://artgrid.io) — $25/mo (premium cinematic)
- [Storyblocks](https://www.storyblocks.com) — $30/mo (supplement-specific)

**Budget**: $0-100 (use free stock for initial tests, upgrade to premium if VSL wins).

## Graphics & Animations

### Required Graphics
1. **Mechanism Explainer** (1:00-2:00 section)
   - Blood plasma vs seawater comparison chart
   - 78 minerals visual (periodic table style)
   - Absorption pathway diagram (simple, not medical jargon)

2. **Social Proof Overlays** (2:00-3:00 section)
   - Customer quote cards (text only, not video testimonials)
   - Data visualizations (HRV graphs, energy charts)
   - Trust badges (30-day guarantee, money-back)

3. **Offer Graphics** (3:00-3:30 section)
   - Price comparison table (LMNT vs IonWave)
   - Subscription benefits checklist
   - CTA button animation

### Animation Complexity
- **Tier 1 (Simple)**: Text animations, fade-ins, slide-ins — $0-50 (Canva, CapCut)
- **Tier 2 (Moderate)**: Motion graphics, icon animations, transitions — $100-200 (Fiverr, Upwork)
- **Tier 3 (Complex)**: 3D animations, particle effects, custom illustrations — $300-500 (professional motion designer)

**IonWave recommendation**: Start Tier 1 for initial tests. Upgrade to Tier 2 if VSL wins (improves perceived value).

## Audio Specifications

### Voiceover Options
| Option | Cost | Pros | Cons | Best For |
|--------|------|------|------|----------|
| **UGC Creator VO** | $100-250 | Authentic, relatable, fast turnaround | Variable quality | Initial tests |
| **Professional VO Artist** | $200-500 | High quality, multiple takes, studio-grade | Expensive, slower | Winning VSLs |
| **AI Voiceover** | $10-50/mo (ElevenLabs, Descript) | Cheap, instant, unlimited edits | Can sound robotic | Hook tests only |
| **Founder VO** | $0 (DIY) | Authentic, no licensing issues | Requires good mic setup | Phase 2+ (post-validation) |

**IonWave Phase 1 recommendation**: UGC creator VO ($100-250). Sounds natural, converts well for supplement VSLs.

### Music Licensing
- **Epidemic Sound** — $15/mo (unlimited royalty-free music)
- **Artlist** — $16/mo (high-quality cinematic tracks)
- **Free**: YouTube Audio Library (limited selection, lower quality)

**Music style for IonWave**: Calm, scientific, aspirational. NOT high-energy gym music (doesn't match marine plasma positioning).

**Budget**: $15-30/mo for music subscription.

## Platform Format Specifications

| Platform | Aspect Ratio | Length | Captions | Notes |
|----------|-------------|--------|----------|-------|
| **Meta (Feed)** | 1:1 (square) or 4:5 (vertical) | 3-5 min | MANDATORY | 85% watch with sound off |
| **Meta (Stories)** | 9:16 (vertical) | 15-60 sec | MANDATORY | Short hook tests only |
| **TikTok** | 9:16 (vertical) | 1-3 min | Optional (text overlays work better) | Faster pacing, trending sounds |
| **YouTube** | 16:9 (horizontal) | 3-10 min | Optional | Longer VSLs tolerated |
| **Instagram Reels** | 9:16 (vertical) | 30-90 sec | MANDATORY | Hook + CTA only (drive to link in bio) |

**Export settings** (use across all platforms):
- Video codec: H.264
- Audio codec: AAC
- Frame rate: 30fps (or 60fps for premium feel)
- Bitrate: 10-15 Mbps (high quality without massive file size)

**IonWave priority**: Start with Meta 1:1 (widest reach). Add TikTok 9:16 if Meta performs well.

## FTC Compliance Checklist (MANDATORY)

Before producing ANY health/supplement VSL:

- [ ] **Health claims substantiation** — Every claim backed by RCT or structure/function language
- [ ] **NO fictional testimonials** — Marcus T., Jennifer R., David K. are fictional (CANNOT USE)
- [ ] **Disclose material connections** — If UGC creator was paid, must disclose in video
- [ ] **Typical results disclaimer** — If showing atypical results (HRV +15 points), must say "results not typical"
- [ ] **Before/after photo compliance** — If using, must be real customer with signed release
- [ ] **Comparative claims substantiation** — If saying "better than LMNT," must have head-to-head test data
- [ ] **Money-back guarantee terms** — Must honor 30-day refund (no "processing fees" loopholes)
- [ ] **Affiliate disclosure** — If influencer/UGC creator has affiliate link, must disclose prominently

**FTC penalty**: $53,088+ per violation. One bad VSL can cost 6-figures in fines.

[Confidence: A | Source: FTC Health Products Compliance Guidance (Dec 2022), Consumer Review Rule | Date: 2026-02-09]

### IonWave-Specific Compliance Flags

**Claims to AVOID** (unsubstantiated):
- "98% bioavailable" (no RCT proof)
- "Cure fatigue / brain fog / recovery issues" (disease claims, illegal)
- "Same as IV therapy" (medical device comparison, illegal)

**Claims OK to USE** (structure/function):
- "Supports healthy hydration"
- "Helps maintain mineral balance"
- "Derived from ocean water"
- "78 naturally-occurring minerals"

**Testimonial compliance**:
- Use ONLY real post-launch customer testimonials
- Include disclaimer: "Individual results vary. Not typical outcomes."
- NO before/after health metrics unless backed by signed release + medical data

## Production Timeline

| Phase | Duration | Deliverables | Owner |
|-------|----------|-------------|-------|
| **Script Lock** | 1-2 days | Finalized script approved by compliance | Copywriter + Legal |
| **Pre-Production** | 3-5 days | Shot list, creator booked, B-roll sourced | Producer |
| **Production** | 1-2 days | Raw footage captured | UGC Creator |
| **Post-Production** | 3-5 days | Edited master + 3 platform variants | Video Editor |
| **Graphics/Animations** | 2-3 days (parallel) | Motion graphics, overlays | Motion Designer |
| **Review & Revisions** | 1-2 days | Final QA, compliance double-check | Producer + Legal |
| **Export & Upload** | 1 day | Platform-specific exports, Meta/TikTok upload | Media Buyer |

**Total**: 10-14 days from script lock to live VSL.
