## Calibration Rationale

**Why Conservative Adjustment (-0.38 average)?**

1. **Pre-Validation Status:** M4 is built for Week 0 (pre-outreach, pre-investor feedback). All recommendations are theory + Danilo's bootstrap sources, not battle-tested with IonWave investors. Risk of over-confidence if framework looks perfect on paper but breaks in practice.

2. **Experimental Components:** Trade-as-pitch is D-grade confidence (completely untested). Including it in M4 without validation may inflate quality perception.

3. **Missing Hard Data:** TAM/SAM/SOM has placeholder, micro-raise comparables are scarce, Danilo's background not fully detailed. These gaps don't block execution but reduce evidence robustness.

4. **Optimism Bias:** Self-grading tends toward generosity ("this looks good to me" ≠ "this is objectively excellent"). Conservative adjustment counteracts inherent bias.

**Result:** Raw 8.8 → Calibrated 8.4 (7% downward adjustment, within recommended 5-10% calibration range for pre-validation TUPs)
