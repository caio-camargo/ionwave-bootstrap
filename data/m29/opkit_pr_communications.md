## How to Use This OpKit (Step-by-Step)

### For a New Trade (any bootstrapped DTC brand):

**Step 1: Customize the Future Press Release**
- Open `pr_strategy.md` Section 1
- Replace IonWave specifics with your brand, product, and metrics
- Fill in the "What Must Be True" table with your specific preconditions
- This becomes your strategic planning artifact — review quarterly

**Step 2: Write Your Milestone Press Releases**
- Adapt the 4 milestone templates (Section 2) to your milestones
- These are for internal alignment and investor updates — don't distribute to media

**Step 3: Set Up Your Media Outreach**
- Create accounts on Qwoted and Featured.com (Section 3.2)
- Build your own Tier 1 (achievable) and Tier 2 (aspirational) media target lists
- Begin daily expert quote platform routine (15-30 min/day)
- Follow the phase-gated timeline (Section 4)

**Step 4: Adapt the Video Strategy**
- Review the phased rollout (Section 2) — defer YouTube to Month 4-6
- Start with short-form video (TikTok/Reels) earlier if applicable
- Customize content pillars for your product category

**Step 5: Customize the Crisis Playbook**
- Review all 8 scenarios — remove any that don't apply
- Add industry-specific scenarios if needed
- Complete the pre-crisis preparedness checklist (Section 5) BEFORE launch
- Identify legal counsel before you need them

**Step 6: Evaluate Podcast (Month 6+)**
- Only open `podcast_strategy.md` when you have 5+ podcast guest appearances
- Evaluate using the assessment framework in Section 1

## Key Principles

1. **PR is a multiplier, not a channel** — it amplifies other channels (SEO, ads, email) but doesn't replace them
2. **Backlinks are the real ROI** — for a bootstrapped brand, PR's #1 value is high-DA backlinks that accelerate SEO
3. **Start small, work up** — pitch niche blogs before national outlets, guest on small podcasts before A-listers
4. **Founder Mode** — a solo founder gets 2-3 hrs/week for PR, not more. Budget time accordingly.
5. **Crisis prep beats crisis response** — complete the pre-crisis checklist before launch, not after the first fire
6. **Defer video and podcast** — blog content first (highest ROI per hour). YouTube at Month 4-6. Podcast at Month 8+.
