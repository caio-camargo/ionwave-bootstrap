## Dialogue Rounds

### ROUND 1
**CHALLENGE** (SI): PR at zero revenue has zero ROI — why dedicate time to it when ads and content have provable returns?
**RESPONSE** (BS): PR's value is backlinks (SEO) and social proof ("As seen in..."), not direct traffic.
**SYNTHESIS** (OE): Both valid, but the real issue is time allocation — PR + content + ads already exceeds a solo founder's bandwidth.
**OUTCOME**: UPGRADED (U1)
**ACTION**: Added weekly PR time budget capped at 2-3 hrs/week in Phase 1, with ROI framed through SEO backlink value.

### ROUND 2
**CHALLENGE** (BS): Podcast strategy over-plans a Phase 3+ initiative — 10 episode concepts and full production plan create the illusion it should happen sooner.
**RESPONSE** (OE): Agreed. File should be 50% shorter, focused on "guesting first" and compressed production appendix.
**SYNTHESIS** (SI): Podcast metrics are fantasy — 500-1,500 downloads/episode assumes 20+ episodes published, but only 8-10 will exist by Month 12. Revise to 100-300.
**OUTCOME**: UPGRADED (U2)
**ACTION**: Added "DO NOT START YET" warning to podcast_strategy.md. Revised download targets down 40-50%. Shortened production details.

### ROUND 3
**CHALLENGE** (OE): Crisis playbook is strong but missing competitive claim copying scenario — what if a competitor starts using "78 minerals" or "marine plasma" language?
**RESPONSE** (SI): That's a legal question (trademark), not just PR. Reference M7 for IP protection.
**SYNTHESIS** (BS): Add a brief scenario focused on comms response: double down on content moat, don't publicly attack competitor.
**OUTCOME**: UPGRADED (U3)
**ACTION**: Added Scenario 3.8 (Competitive Claim Copying) to crisis_playbook.md.

### ROUND 4
**CHALLENGE** (SI): Media target list is all household-name publications (Healthline, Men's Health). A pre-revenue startup won't get featured there. Where are the achievable targets?
**RESPONSE** (BS): Keep the aspirational list, but add a Tier 1 of small/niche outlets where a new brand can realistically get featured.
**SYNTHESIS** (OE): Same logic as podcast guesting — "start small, work up."
**OUTCOME**: UPGRADED (U4)
**ACTION**: Added Tier 1 (achievable) media targets: keto blogs, supplement review sites, local press, DTC/startup publications, trade press.

### ROUND 5
**CHALLENGE** (BS): Future press release is good but "What Must Be True" doesn't prioritize. What's the minimum viable press pitch — what needs to be true before ANY outreach?
**RESPONSE** (SI): Add a 3-tier checklist: must-have, nice-to-have, dream tier. Product shipping + 20 reviews + professional website is the floor.
**SYNTHESIS** (OE): Satisfied with content quality overall. Phase-gated timeline is realistic. Time budgets are honest.
**OUTCOME**: UPGRADED (U5)
**ACTION**: Added "Minimum Viable Press Pitch" checklist with 3 tiers to pr_strategy.md.
