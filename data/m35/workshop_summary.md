## Key Innovations

1. **Choreography Framework**: Sequencing loops so they feed forward (daily → weekly → monthly → quarterly). Not just "have meetings," but design how they inform each other.

2. **Phase-Gated Strictness**: Objective LAUNCH → PMF → SCALE gates (LTV:CAC >3.0, NPS >40%, churn <15%) prevent premature scaling. Stricter than industry standard.

3. **Crisis Mode Protocol**: Normal rhythms PAUSE when existential blocker hits (ad account ban, cash <30 days, churn >30%). Circuit breaker rule prevents blindly executing stale plan.

4. **Hour-by-Hour Week 1**: Day-by-Day plan is actionable granularity (e.g., "9:00-10:00 call lawyer, 10:00-11:00 Mercury application"). Founder can execute without asking "what does this mean?"

5. **Kill Discipline**: WBR has "Kills & Pivots" section forcing weekly decision on underperformers. Decision logging prevents re-trying failed experiments 6 months later.

6. **Pre-Launch Supplier Work**: Dialogue revealed CoA timing issue (10-14 days, not 24-48 hrs). Added "Week -2" pre-work so CoA is in hand by Day 1 (prevents Day 7 checkpoint failure).

7. **Dual LTV Gate**: PMF gate is LTV:CAC >3.0 AND Month 3 retention >60% (protects against LTV overstating from churn spike in Month 4-6).
