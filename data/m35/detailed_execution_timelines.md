## 6-Month Operating Plan (Day 1-180)

**Purpose**: Bridge between 30/90/365 high-level plans and week-by-week tactical execution
**Audience**: Founder + team (if hired)
**Update Frequency**: Monthly refresh (roll forward 6 months)

---

### Month 1 (Day 1-30): FOUNDATION & LAUNCH

**Objective**: Go from zero to live store with first 10 orders
**Budget**: $5K-10K (product, setup, light ad spend)
**Team**: Caio (solo founder)

**Key Milestones**:
- Day 7: Entity live, CoA received, store technically ready
- Day 14: Creative assets ready, marketing infrastructure live
- Day 21: Soft launch (warm network)
- Day 30: First 10 orders, CAC validated

**Weekly Themes**:
- **Week 1**: Foundation (ODD-5 execution: entity, product, technical)
- **Week 2**: Creative production (brand, ads, landing page)
- **Week 3**: Soft launch (friends/family, $100-300 test spend)
- **Week 4**: Iteration (kill underperformers, optimize, prepare Month 2 ramp)

**Risks**:
- Supplier delay (CoA late, batch delayed)
- Technical issues (tracking broken, checkout errors)
- Creative production bottleneck (no photographer, designer unavailable)

---

### Month 2 (Day 31-60): ACQUISITION RAMP

**Objective**: $3K-8K revenue, 30-100 orders, validated unit economics
**Budget**: $2K-5K ad spend
**Team**: Caio (solo) + potential contractor (UGC creator, VA for fulfillment)

**Key Milestones**:
- Day 45: $1500-3000 Meta spend deployed, CAC <$60
- Day 60: First repeat purchase data (Month 1 cohort reorder rate)

**Weekly Themes**:
- **Week 5-6**: Channel validation (Meta primary, TikTok test, Google test)
- **Week 7-8**: Retention focus (cohort tracking, email optimization, referral activation)

**Risks**:
- CAC spike (creative fatigue, CPM increase)
- Fulfillment breaking (>50 orders/week, founder time constraint)
- Cash flow squeeze (ad spend + inventory restock)

---

### Month 3 (Day 61-90): OPERATIONAL MATURITY

**Objective**: $5K-12K revenue, 50-150 orders, PMF hypothesis test
**Budget**: $3K-8K ad spend
**Team**: Caio + potential part-time hire (CX or ads)

**Key Milestones**:
- Day 75: 3PL quotes finalized if self-fulfillment breaking
- Day 90: **LAUNCH → PMF Gate** (pass/fail decision)

**Weekly Themes**:
- **Week 9-10**: Efficiency (3PL eval, financial dashboards, creative rotation)
- **Week 11-12**: PMF testing (NPS survey, cohort analysis, product iteration)

**Risks**:
- PMF gate fail (churn high, CAC unsustainable, retention weak)
- Operational gaps (self-fulfillment breaking, founder burnout)

---

### Month 4 (Day 91-120): PMF VALIDATION START

**Objective**: $10K-20K revenue, prove reproducibility
**Budget**: $5K-12K ad spend
**Team**: Caio + Ads Specialist (part-time or contractor)

**Key Milestones**:
- Day 100: First hire onboarded (Ads or CX)
- Day 120: LTV:CAC approaching 3.0

**Weekly Themes**:
- **Week 13-14**: Hiring + diversification (team ramp, TikTok/Google scale)
- **Week 15-16**: Product iteration (flavor SKU test if feedback warrants)

**Risks**:
- Hiring miss (wrong person, slow ramp)
- Channel plateau (Meta fatigue, no scalable backup)

---

### Month 5 (Day 121-150): PMF VALIDATION MID

**Objective**: $12K-25K revenue, subscription revenue >25% of total
**Budget**: $8K-15K ad spend
**Team**: Caio + Ads + CX (if volume >100 tickets/month)

**Key Milestones**:
- Day 135: Influencer partnerships (2-3 paid deals if seeding worked)
- Day 150: Cohort retention stabilizing (Month 3 cohort >60% active)

**Weekly Themes**:
- **Week 17-18**: Influencer scale, content SEO ramp
- **Week 19-20**: Financial ops tightening (P&L review, cash forecast)

**Risks**:
- Influencer ROAS disappointing (<1.5x)
- Churn acceleration (product quality issue, market saturation)

---

### Month 6 (Day 151-180): PMF VALIDATION END

**Objective**: $15K-30K revenue, **PMF → SCALE Gate** decision
**Budget**: $10K-20K ad spend
**Team**: Caio + Ads + CX + (optional Ops Coordinator)

**Key Milestones**:
- Day 165: 3PL transition if volume >200 orders/month
- Day 180: **PMF → SCALE Gate** (pass/fail/iterate decision)

**Weekly Themes**:
- **Week 21-22**: Operational prep (3PL, SOPs, inventory model)
- **Week 23-24**: Gate assessment (run PMF checklist, QBR planning)

**Risks**:
- PMF gate fail (LTV:CAC <3.0, churn >15%, NPS <40%)
- Cash runway tight (need fundraising or revenue ramp)

## Week-by-Week Narrative (First 12 Weeks)

**Format**: Prose narrative with tactical focus for each week
**Purpose**: Help founder visualize the arc of the first 90 days

---

### Week 1: "The Sprint to Live"

**Mon-Tue**: Entity formation, bank account, QBO setup. Call lawyer for expedited Delaware filing. Set up Mercury account (same-day approval likely). Configure QBO with M25 Day 1 Essentials chart. Total time: 6-8 hours.

**Wed-Fri**: Supplier coordination. Email 3 marine plasma suppliers (Biocean, Actimar, local options). Request CoA, lead time, MOQ, pricing. Place order with whoever responds fastest with compliant CoA (heavy metals <10ppb As). Order 500-1000 sachets ($240-480 COGS). Packaging design kickoff (if designer hired) or Canva DIY with FDA template. Total time: 8-10 hours.

**Sat-Sun**: Shopify setup blitz. Install theme (Dawn or paid theme <$300), configure settings, add product, set up Recharge subscription, test checkout. Install GA4, Meta Pixel, configure CAPI. Total time: 6-8 hours.

**Week 1 Win Condition**: Entity live, product ordered with CoA confirmed, store technically ready. If CoA is non-compliant, DO NOT PROCEED — find new supplier.

---

### Week 2: "Creative Crunch"

**Mon-Tue**: Brand finalization. Logo (hire on Fiverr $50-200 if needed, or use Canva template). Lock color palette, typography, voice guide. Total time: 4-6 hours.

**Wed-Thu**: Photography. If budget allows, hire product photographer ($300-800 for half-day shoot). If DIY, use iPhone + natural light + simple props. Get 5-10 product shots (sachet, box, lifestyle, detail). Total time: 4-8 hours (shoot + editing).

**Fri-Sat**: Ad creative production. Write 3-5 hooks (see M11 hook library). Record 1 VSL (iPhone, Caio to camera, 60-90 sec). Create 2-4 static ads (Canva + product photos). Total time: 6-8 hours.

**Sun**: Landing page build. Shopify page builder (Gempages/Pagefly or native). Hero (hook + product image), benefits (3-5 bullets), FAQ, compliance disclaimers. Test on mobile. Total time: 4-6 hours.

**Week 2 Win Condition**: Creative assets ready, LP live, ads uploaded to Meta. If photography fails, proceed with stock + Canva graphics (not ideal but unblocking).

---

### Week 3: "Soft Launch"

**Mon**: Email warm network. "Hey, I'm launching IonWave — marine plasma electrolytes. Would love your feedback. Here's 20% off [link]." Send to 50-100 people (friends, family, LinkedIn, Twitter). Total time: 2 hours.

**Tue-Wed**: Test ads. $50/day Meta spend (3 ads, $15-20 each). Monitor hourly Day 1 (check tracking works, no errors). Total time: 3-4 hours setup + monitoring.

**Thu-Fri**: First orders. Fulfill 3-10 orders (pack, ship, upload tracking). Email each customer personally: "Thanks for being first! How's it taste?" Total time: 2-3 hours fulfillment + 1 hour customer emails.

**Sat-Sun**: Data review. GA4, Meta Ads Manager, Shopify. Revenue, orders, CAC, tracking validation. If tracking broken (revenue mismatch >20%), fix immediately. If CAC >$100, pause ads, diagnose (targeting too broad? Creative bad? Checkout friction?). Total time: 3-4 hours.

**Week 3 Win Condition**: 3-10 orders placed, tracking validated, zero critical failures. If <3 orders from warm network + $150 spend, demand signal weak — reassess messaging or ICP.

---

### Week 4: "Iterate or Die"

**Mon-Tue**: Kill underperformers. Any ad with <1 conversion after $30 spend → pause. Any ad with ROAS <1.0 → pause. Reallocate budget to winner (if any). Total time: 2-3 hours.

**Wed-Thu**: LP optimization. If conversion <1.5%, test changes: headline, CTA button text, add urgency timer, simplify checkout (remove optional fields). A/B test with Shopify native or Google Optimize replacement (Shoplift/Intelligems). Total time: 3-4 hours.

**Fri**: Product feedback. If 3+ customers say "too salty," note for flavor SKU. If packaging complaints ("hard to tear"), note for v2. If quality issues ("clumpy," "smells off"), escalate to supplier. Total time: 2 hours.

**Sat-Sun**: Month 2 prep. Scale budget to $500-1000/week if CAC <$50. Plan creative refresh (1-2 new hooks). Schedule email flows (cart recovery, welcome). Total time: 4-5 hours.

**Week 4 Win Condition**: 10-30 orders cumulative, CAC <$60, ready to ramp Month 2. If CAC >$80 or <10 orders total, Month 2 is slower iteration, not aggressive scale.

---

### Weeks 5-12: Abbreviated Narrative

**Week 5-6**: Channel validation. Meta primary $1500-3000/month. TikTok $300-500 test. Google brand terms $200. Creative refresh (2-4 new ads). Email cart recovery live.

**Week 7-8**: Retention focus. Cohort tracking starts. Post-purchase email flow (Day 7, 14, 21). Referral program activated. First repeat purchase measured (target >5%).

**Week 9-10**: Efficiency gains. 3PL quotes if self-fulfillment >50 orders/week. Financial dashboards (Survival Five). Creative library rotation (kill fatigued, scale winners).

**Week 11-12**: PMF testing. NPS survey to Month 1-3 cohorts. Cohort retention analysis. Product iteration if feedback strong. Financial model updated with actuals. **Day 90 Gate decision.**

## Week 1 Day-by-Day Plan (ODD-5 Execution)

**Purpose**: Granular daily checklist for the most critical week
**Owner**: Caio (founder)
**Format**: Hour-by-hour where precision matters, otherwise task blocks

---

### Day 1 (Monday): Entity & Banking

**Morning (9 AM - 12 PM)**:
- [ ] 9:00-10:00: Call lawyer (or use Stripe Atlas / Firstbase.io for DIY). Start Delaware C-Corp formation. Confirm EIN application same-day.
- [ ] 10:00-11:00: Apply for Mercury/Relay/Brex bank account (online application, 15-30 min). Upload EIN docs when ready.
- [ ] 11:00-12:00: QBO setup. Create account (free trial or $30/month). Configure Day 1 Essentials chart of accounts (18 accounts from M25). Connect bank when approved.

**Afternoon (1 PM - 5 PM)**:
- [ ] 1:00-2:30: Shopify account creation. $1 trial or $79/month plan. Domain purchased (ionwave.com or alternative). SSL enabled.
- [ ] 2:30-4:00: Theme installation. Download Dawn (free) or purchase premium theme (<$300). Install, configure basic settings (logo placeholder, colors, fonts).
- [ ] 4:00-5:00: Product listing. Add "IonWave Marine Plasma Electrolytes" product. Placeholder image, description, $44 price, 30-sachet variant.

**Evening (Optional)**:
- [ ] 6:00-7:00: Supplier outreach. Email 3 suppliers: "Looking for marine plasma, need CoA with heavy metals <10ppb As, <5ppb Pb, <3ppb Hg. MOQ 500-1000 sachets. Lead time?" Track responses in Google Sheet.

**Day 1 Win**: Entity in progress, bank applied, Shopify live with placeholder product.

---

### Day 2 (Tuesday): Compliance & Product

**Morning (9 AM - 12 PM)**:
- [ ] 9:00-10:00: Follow up on entity formation. Confirm EIN received. Upload to Mercury/Relay.
- [ ] 10:00-12:00: FDA compliance research. Read M7 DSHEA section. Draft Supplement Facts Panel (use FDA template or Canva). List ingredients: Ocean minerals (Na, K, Mg, trace), purified water. Add required disclaimer: "These statements have not been evaluated by the FDA. This product is not intended to diagnose, treat, cure, or prevent any disease."

**Afternoon (1 PM - 5 PM)**:
- [ ] 1:00-3:00: Supplier coordination. Review CoA from responses (if any). Check heavy metal limits. If compliant, request quote for 500 sachets + packaging. If non-compliant, reject and continue search.
- [ ] 3:00-4:30: Packaging design. If hiring designer, brief them (Supplement Facts Panel, Prop 65 warning if CA, brand logo, usage instructions). If DIY, Canva template. Target: sachet design + box design.
- [ ] 4:30-5:00: Trademark search. USPTO.gov TESS search for "IonWave" in Class 5 (dietary supplements). If clear, note to file application Week 2. If conflicted, consider alternative name.

**Evening (Optional)**:
- [ ] 6:00-7:00: Privacy Policy / Terms. Use Shopify template generator or Termly.io (free tier). Customize for GDPR/CCPA. Add DSHEA disclaimer to Terms.

**Day 2 Win**: CoA evaluation in progress, packaging design started, compliance docs drafted.

---

### Day 3 (Wednesday): Technical Setup Part 1

**Morning (9 AM - 12 PM)**:
- [ ] 9:00-10:30: GA4 setup. Create GA4 property, add tracking code to Shopify (Preferences → Google Analytics). Test (visit site, check real-time traffic in GA4).
- [ ] 10:30-12:00: Meta Pixel + CAPI. Create Meta Business Manager, add Pixel, install Shopify Facebook channel app. Configure CAPI (Shopify native or Elevar/Triple Whale app $50-200/month). Test pixel with Pixel Helper extension.

**Afternoon (1 PM - 5 PM)**:
- [ ] 1:00-2:30: Recharge/Skio setup. Install subscription app. Configure: 30-day supply, "Subscribe & Save 15%" discount, toggle UI (not dropdown — M15 best practice). Test subscription checkout (place test order, cancel immediately).
- [ ] 2:30-4:00: Payment processor. Enable Shopify Payments (or Stripe if Shopify Payments unavailable in country). Set fraud rules: Auto-decline if AVS mismatch + CVV fail. Set spend cap 2x daily budget in Meta (prevents ad hijacking — M9).
- [ ] 4:00-5:00: Consent Mode v2. Install Shopify consent app or manual Google Tag Manager setup. GDPR/CCPA cookie banner. Test (visit site incognito, see banner, accept, verify GA4 + Pixel fire).

**Evening (Optional)**:
- [ ] 6:00-7:00: Test checkout end-to-end. Place test order (one-time + subscription). Verify: payment processes, confirmation email sent, order shows in Shopify, GA4 purchase event fires, Meta purchase event fires. If anything broken, debug immediately.

**Day 3 Win**: Tracking validated, subscription working, payment processor live.

---

### Day 4 (Thursday): Technical Setup Part 2 & Product Order

**Morning (9 AM - 12 PM)**:
- [ ] 9:00-10:00: Email platform. Klaviyo free tier (up to 250 contacts) or Mailchimp. Connect to Shopify. Configure: Welcome flow (trigger: first order), Cart Abandonment flow (trigger: checkout started, not completed, 1-hour delay).
- [ ] 10:00-11:30: Referral app. Loop/Smile install. Configure: "Give 10%, Get 10%" for referrer and referee. Test (place order, check referral link generated).
- [ ] 11:30-12:00: Final supplier decision. Lock supplier with compliant CoA. Place order for 500-1000 sachets. Confirm lead time (target: 7-14 days delivery). Pre-pay or 50% deposit if required.

**Afternoon (1 PM - 5 PM)**:
- [ ] 1:00-3:00: Theme performance audit. M9 performance budget: <500KB JS, <3 external scripts, <8 apps. Remove unnecessary apps. Test load speed (PageSpeed Insights, target >70 mobile score). If slow, simplify theme or upgrade to faster theme.
- [ ] 3:00-5:00: Inventory setup. Shopify → Products → Inventory. Track quantity. Note: When sachets arrive, update inventory to actual count. Set "low stock" alert at 100 sachets (reorder trigger).

**Evening (Optional)**:
- [ ] 6:00-7:00: Shipping materials order. Amazon/Uline: poly mailers (100-500 count, $20-50), packing tape, shipping labels (if not using Shopify Shipping). Estimate arrival Day 10-12.

**Day 4 Win**: Supplier order placed, email/referral infrastructure live, performance optimized.

---

### Day 5 (Friday): Brand & Creative Kickoff

**Morning (9 AM - 12 PM)**:
- [ ] 9:00-11:00: Logo finalization. If hiring designer (Fiverr/Upwork), post job ("Marine wellness brand logo, clean/modern, $100-200 budget, 48-hour delivery"). If DIY, Canva logo maker. Lock 2-3 concepts.
- [ ] 11:00-12:00: Brand voice guide. Write 1-page doc: Archetype = Informed Guide (M8). Tone: science-backed not academic, explain don't gatekeep, transparency-first, conversational-professional. 5 examples: "Marine plasma delivers 78 minerals in their natural, ionic form — the same ratio as human blood" (YES) vs. "Proprietary mineral blend scientifically formulated for peak performance" (NO — vague).

**Afternoon (1 PM - 5 PM)**:
- [ ] 1:00-3:00: Photography planning. If hiring photographer, scout on Instagram/local FB groups. Book for Week 2 (Day 10-12). Brief: product shots (sachet, box, lifestyle), white background + natural light, 10-15 finals. Budget: $300-800.
- [ ] 3:00-5:00: Ad creative research. M11/M12 swipe file. Browse Meta Ad Library (LMNT, Liquid IV, AG1). Screenshot 10-20 electrolyte ads. Note hooks, formats, CTAs. Adapt for IonWave (don't copy, inspiration only).

**Evening (Optional)**:
- [ ] 6:00-8:00: Draft first ad hooks. Write 5-10 variations:
   1. "78 minerals from the ocean. 30 seconds to dissolve. Zero BS."
   2. "Your body is 70% water. So is the ocean. Coincidence?"
   3. "LMNT has 3 minerals. We have 78. Here's why that matters..."
   4. "Fasting? Keto? Carnivore? You're missing this one thing."
   5. "Why synthetic electrolytes only absorb 10-15%. Ocean minerals: 66-90%."

**Day 5 Win**: Logo in progress, brand voice locked, photography planned, ad hooks drafted.

---

### Day 6-7 (Weekend): Landing Page Build

**Saturday**:
- [ ] 9:00-12:00: LP structure. Hero section: Headline (hook), subhead (benefit), product image, CTA ("Add to Cart"). Benefits section: 3-5 bullets with icons. FAQ section: 5-8 questions (taste, ingredients, shipping, subscription). Compliance: DSHEA disclaimer, return policy, Prop 65 (if CA).
- [ ] 1:00-4:00: LP build. Shopify page builder or Gempages/Pagefly. Mobile-first (60-70% traffic will be mobile). Test on iPhone, Android. CTA buttons large, easy to tap. Subscription toggle visible above fold.
- [ ] 4:00-6:00: Copywriting. Write sections:
   - **Hero**: "Marine Plasma Electrolytes. 78 Minerals. Ocean-Sourced. Zero Synthetic BS."
   - **Benefits**: "Complete Mineralization" (not just Na/K/Mg), "Rapid Absorption" (ionic form), "Fasting-Friendly" (zero calories), "Ocean-Sourced Purity" (no synthetic fillers).
   - **FAQ**: "What's in it?" (ocean minerals, purified water), "How does it taste?" (lightly salty, ocean-like), "Is it safe?" (third-party tested, CoA available), "Can I cancel subscription?" (yes, anytime, one-click).

**Sunday**:
- [ ] 9:00-12:00: Compliance final check. DSHEA disclaimer visible on LP. Prop 65 warning (if CA) or link to CoA. FTC Negative Option Rule: subscription terms clear ("Cancel anytime, no fees"). Return policy (30-day money-back). Privacy policy link in footer.
- [ ] 1:00-3:00: LP testing. Place test order (one-time + subscription). Check mobile experience. Click every link (FAQ expands, footer links work). Load speed (PageSpeed Insights >70 mobile).
- [ ] 3:00-5:00: Ad account setup. Meta Business Manager: Create ad account, add payment method (credit card or PayPal), verify business (upload EIN docs if required). TikTok Ads Manager: Create account, add payment, verify. Google Ads: Create account, add payment.

**Day 6-7 Win**: Landing page live with compliance, ad accounts ready to launch Week 2.

## Touchpoint Cadence (External Stakeholders)

**Purpose**: Proactive communication schedule to avoid "went dark" perception

---

### Customers

| Touchpoint | Trigger | Channel | Template |
|------------|---------|---------|----------|
| Order Confirmation | Order placed | Email (auto) | "Thanks for your order! Tracking: [link]" |
| Shipping Notification | Order shipped | Email (auto) | "Your IonWave is on the way! Track: [link]" |
| Day 7 Check-In | 7 days post-delivery | Email (auto) | "How's it going? We'd love your feedback." |
| Day 14 Feedback | 14 days post-delivery | Email (auto) + manual if high-value | "Quick survey: How would you rate IonWave? [1-5 scale]" |
| Subscription Renewal | 3 days before charge | Email (auto) | "Your next shipment: $44 on [date]. Pause/skip/cancel anytime." |
| Pause Outreach | Subscription paused | Email (auto) + SMS <24hr to cancel | "We noticed you paused. Anything we can improve?" |

---

### Investors (if fundraising active, M4)

| Touchpoint | Frequency | Format | Owner |
|------------|-----------|--------|-------|
| Monthly Update | First Friday of month | Email (1-2 pages) | Caio |

- Metrics: Revenue, orders, CAC, LTV:CAC, churn
- Wins: Major milestones, customer testimonials, press
