## Photo Brief

### Photography Style

**Tone**: Clean, modern, aspirational (not clinical or overly lifestyle)
**Lighting**: Natural light preferred, soft shadows, bright but not blown-out
**Color Grading**: Cool tones (ocean blues, teals), desaturated slightly for calm feel
**Composition**: Rule of thirds, negative space, product as hero

**Reference Brands**:
- Glossier (clean, minimal, product-focused)
- Patagonia (authentic, environmental)
- Momentous (science-backed without sterile)

## Unboxing Experience (Detailed Flow)

### Touchpoint 1: Shipping Notification Email

**Subject**: "Your IonWave is on the way"
**Body**: Tracking link + "Here's what to expect when it arrives" (link to unboxing guide)
**Tone**: Excited, welcoming

---

### Touchpoint 2: Mailer Exterior

**Visual**: Kraft mailer, simple IonWave logo (1-color), "Thank you for choosing complete mineralization" on seal strip
**Feel**: Understated, sustainable, premium-but-not-flashy

---

### Touchpoint 3: Opening Mailer

**Contents**:
1. 30-count box (ocean blue, soft-touch matte)
2. Welcome card (4×6", recyclable cardstock, founder message or brand story)
3. Referral card ("Give 20%, Get 20%" promo code)

**Sequence**: Box on top (hero), cards underneath

---

### Touchpoint 4: Opening Box

**Inside Lid**: "Welcome to complete mineralization. Here's what you just opened: 78 minerals from 30 meters below the Atlantic. Same ratios as your blood. Your cells will recognize every one. — The IonWave Team" + QR code (ionwave.com/start)

**Sachets**: Standing upright in 3 rows of 10, organized, easy to pull

---

### Touchpoint 5: First Use

**Sachet Tear**: Notch at top, clean tear, no spilling
**Mixing**: Pour into 8-12oz water, stir/shake, dissolves clear (or light tint if flavored in Phase 2+)
**Taste**: Ocean-salted (natural mineral taste, not sweet), clean finish

---

### Touchpoint 6: Post-Consumption (Digital)

**QR Code Landing Page** (ionwave.com/start):
- Welcome video (30 sec, founder or team member)
- Getting started guide (how to use, what to expect, timeline)
- Full mineral list + sourcing story
- FAQ (most common questions)
- Community link (private Facebook group or Discord, Phase 2+)

[Confidence: B | Source: D2C unboxing best practices (Dollar Shave Club, AG1, Function of Beauty)]

## Competitive Brand Audit (Summary)

| Brand | Strength We Learn From | Weakness We Avoid |
|-------|------------------------|-------------------|
| **LMNT** | Community-first, strong founder, flavor variety | Aggressive/masculine tone (alienates some), only 3 minerals |
| **AG1** | Science-backed via Huberman, NSF-certified trust | $99/month price (barrier), 75 ingredients (complexity) |
| **Momentous** | "Grounded in science," credible partnerships | Less brand personality (functional > emotional) |
| **Quinton** | 125-year heritage, European credibility, gold standard | Medical tone (niche), glass ampoules (inconvenient), $69-90/month |
| **Liquid IV** | Mass retail success, flavor variety, affordable | Only 3 minerals, synthetic, not premium positioning |

**Key Takeaway**: IonWave sits between Quinton (premium marine plasma) and LMNT (accessible electrolytes) — complete ocean mineralization at a daily-use price point.

[Confidence: B | Source: Competitive research 2026-01/02]
