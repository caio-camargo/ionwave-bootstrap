## What Is This?

IonWave is a premium marine plasma electrolyte brand — a health supplement business that sells directly to consumers online.

It's not an idea on a napkin. It has a **complete operating manual**: 41 structured business models covering every function from product formulation to paid advertising to financial operations. Each model is quality-scored, confidence-graded, and designed to be executed by someone who's never met the founder.

---

## The Market

Electrolytes are a proven, growing supplement category. LMNT (the category leader) does $66M+ in annual revenue selling electrolyte packets directly to consumers. The market is real and validated.

**The gap**: The premium end (ocean-sourced minerals, not synthetic) is dominated by one player (Seaonic), and the #1 customer complaint across the category is taste — "jarringly salty," "super salty sewage" (19.4% of all customer feedback). Nobody has solved the taste problem with premium ocean-sourced minerals.

IonWave's bet: flavored marine plasma electrolytes, priced 10-15% below Seaonic, targeting biohackers, keto dieters, endurance athletes, and wellness-focused professionals.

## What Makes This Different

1. **Complete system, not a pitch deck.** 41 TUPs (Trade Unit Projects) covering every operational domain. Whoever executes has a full playbook — not a vision, not a strategy, a system.

2. **AI-augmented execution.** Claude (AI) acts as daily co-pilot. The entire operating manual is structured for AI legibility — the executor can ask Claude about any aspect of the business and get contextualized answers backed by the system's analysis.

3. **De-risked validation.** Kill criteria are defined upfront: if COGS exceeds $0.60, if CAC exceeds $75 for 14 days, if landing page conversion is below 2% after 500 visitors — the business pauses or kills. No ambiguity about when to stop.

4. **Founder-independent.** The founder (Danilo Vicioso) is not in the execution chain. The system is designed so that the right people emerge through the quality of the Trade itself, not through the founder's network.
