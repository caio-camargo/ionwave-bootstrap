## How to Use These Templates

These are starting points, not scripts. **Always personalize** — reference something specific about the candidate or their background. Generic messages get ignored.

Claude can help you personalize: paste in a candidate's profile or background and ask Claude to help you tailor the template.

## Template 1: LinkedIn Connection + Message

**Use when**: Cold outreach to MBA students, business development professionals, or startup community members on LinkedIn.

**Connection request note** (300 char limit):

> Hi [Name] — I'm sourcing for a structured deal-sourcing project with a D2C venture. Your background in [specific: consulting/BD/venture fellowship] caught my eye. Would love to share details if you're open to it.

**Follow-up message** (after connection accepted):

> Thanks for connecting, [Name].

> I'm helping build the team for a premium D2C health brand (marine plasma electrolytes). The venture has a complete operating manual — 41 business models, quality-scored, with AI-augmented execution. It's not a pitch deck; it's a full system.

> We're looking for an MBA intern type to lead the next phase: finding a VP-level deals/fundraising person for the venture. The role involves:

> - Structured outreach to senior professionals
> - Candidate evaluation against defined criteria
> - Pipeline management over 4-12 weeks
> - Working alongside Claude AI as a daily co-pilot

> Compensation is equity-based. Timeline: 4-12 weeks.

> I have a project brief I can share if you're interested. Would a quick call work, or would you prefer to review the materials first?

## Template 4: Freelance Platform Job Posting (Upwork)

**Use when**: Posting to Upwork or similar platforms.

**Title**: MBA-Level Business Development — VP Recruiting for D2C Venture (Equity)

**Description**:

> We're building a premium D2C consumer health brand and need someone to lead a specific, well-defined phase: finding and recruiting a VP-level deals/fundraising person.

> **This is not a general VA role.** We need someone with business development sophistication — the ability to pitch to senior professionals, evaluate candidates against a rubric, and manage a pipeline independently.

> **What you'll do**:
> - Source VP-level candidates across LinkedIn, professional networks, and relevant communities
> - Conduct initial screening conversations using a provided rubric
> - Manage a 4-12 week recruiting pipeline with weekly deliverables
> - Work alongside Claude AI for drafting, evaluation support, and project tracking

> **What you need**:
> - Strong written and verbal communication (you'll be pitching to executives)
> - Experience managing multi-week projects with deliverables
> - Ability to understand and articulate a business opportunity from written materials
> - Comfort with AI-augmented workflows

> **Compensation**: Equity-based. This is not a paid hourly role — it's a project engagement with equity upside in a structured venture. Details provided in the project brief.

> **Timeline**: 4-12 weeks, remote, flexible schedule.

> To apply: Tell me (1) why this interests you, (2) describe a time you managed a recruiting or outreach pipeline, and (3) your availability for the next 3 months.

## Personalization Checklist

Before sending any outreach, verify:

- [ ] Candidate's name is correct (double-check spelling)
- [ ] Referenced something specific from their profile or background
- [ ] Adjusted the pitch angle based on their likely motivations (MBA student → experience/resume; BD professional → deal flow/equity; startup person → venture building)
- [ ] Included a clear next step (call, review materials, etc.)
- [ ] Tone is professional but not stiff

---

## Response Handling

| Response Type | Action |
|---------------|--------|
| **Interested — wants to learn more** | Send the Trade Pitch One-Pager. Schedule a screening conversation (15-20 min). |
| **Interested — has questions first** | Answer questions concisely. Then share the One-Pager and schedule screening. |
| **Maybe / timing concerns** | Acknowledge. Ask about their timeline. Keep in pipeline for follow-up. |
| **Not interested** | Thank them. Ask if they know anyone who might be interested (referral). |
| **No response after follow-up** | Mark as unresponsive. Move on. Don't send more than 2 messages. |
