## Your Role

You have two parallel functions:

1. **Capital Formation** — Raise seed funding, build an advisor network, create an investor pipeline
2. **Find the Operator** — Recruit the person who will execute the business day-to-day

This is an active execution role with deliverables and deadlines. You are not an advisor. You are not a board observer. You make deals happen.

---

## Context: Where You Fit

You are the third node in a recruiting cascade:

```
Danilo (Founder) → VA → MBA Intern → [YOU] → Operator
```

The MBA intern found you. Your job is to raise capital and find the Operator. The Operator will execute the Trade — run the business from entity formation through Day 90 and beyond.

## What You're Working With

### The Trade

IonWave has a **complete operating manual** — 41 structured business models (TUPs) covering every operational domain. You have full access. Key areas for your functions:

- **M0 (Trade Thesis)**: The strategic foundation — why this market, product, and approach
- **M4 (Fundraising)**: Capital Formation playbook — investor targeting, pipeline methodology, objection handling
- **M1 (Labor Chain)**: Operator system — compensation, onboarding, certification, contract
- **M35 (Execution Plans)**: Phase-gated timeline from entity formation to Day 90

### AI Co-Pilot

You work with Claude daily. Claude has the full Trade in context and can:
- Answer questions about any of the 41 TUPs
- Help develop fundraising strategy and pitch materials
- Help evaluate operator candidates against the M1 criteria
- Draft outreach, analyze pipeline, format deliverables
- Track your deadlines and remind you of upcoming deliverables

### The One Constraint

Before proceeding with Capital Formation or Operator recruiting, you must complete the **Integration Work Artifact** (see integration_requirements.md). This is a condensed Trade narrative in your own words — a forcing function to verify you've absorbed the Trade deeply enough to represent it credibly to investors and operator candidates.

## Timeline

- **Weeks 1-2**: Absorb the Trade. Complete integration work artifact. This is your investment in comprehension before action.
- **Week 3**: Submit Capital Formation pipeline plan + Operator sourcing plan. Begin both tracks in parallel.
- **Weeks 3-6**: Active Capital Formation outreach. Active Operator sourcing.
- **Week 6**: First Operator shortlist due.
- **Weeks 7-10**: Deep evaluation of top Operator candidates. Continue Capital Formation.
- **Weeks 8-12**: Operator confirmation, compensation negotiation, onboarding handoff.

Capital Formation is **ongoing** — it doesn't end when the Operator is found. The Operator needs capital to execute. Your fundraising function continues in parallel.

## What You Don't Do

- **Run the business.** That's the Operator's job.
- **Build the product.** The Trade specifies formulation, supply chain, branding.
- **Manage marketing.** The Operator runs the execution playbook.
- **Report to the founder daily.** The compliance agent (Claude) handles monitoring. You deliver on your schedule.

## What You Should Focus On

1. **Integration first.** The Trade is comprehensive. Spend 2 weeks absorbing it. The integration artifact ensures you understand the system you're pitching.
2. **Parallel execution.** Capital Formation and Operator search run simultaneously. Neither waits for the other.
3. **Quality of Operator selection.** The Operator's quality determines IonWave's outcome. This is the most consequential hire in the chain.
4. **Investor conviction.** Investors at seed stage invest in the system and the team. You are part of the team they're betting on.
