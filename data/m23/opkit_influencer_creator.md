## Component 1: Instruction Document

### How to Build an Influencer & Creator Program (14 Steps)

**Phase 0: Foundation (Before Any Outreach)**
1. **Define the distinction**: Influencers post to their audience (awareness). Creators make content for your ads (conversion). Same person can do both, but the deal structure differs.
2. **Build compliance package**: Before touching anyone — approved talking points, prohibited claims, monitoring plan, violation response protocol. NON-NEGOTIABLE for regulated categories (supplements, health, finance).
3. **Set up tracking**: Unique affiliate/promo codes per partner. Attribution tracking for both direct (code use) and indirect (branded search lift). Set up Google Search Console branded query baseline.

**Phase 1: Creator Program (Month 1-3)**
4. **Source 3-5 UGC creators**: Start with platforms (Billo, Insense) or DIY sourcing (Instagram DMs to micro-creators).
5. **Brief creators**: 7-element brief — product info, target audience, video specs, hook options, talking points, do's/don'ts, reference videos.
6. **Manage production cycle**: Brief → Delivery (7 days) → Review (48hrs) → Revisions (max 2) → Approve → Deploy to ads.
7. **Repurpose every piece**: Each video → 4-6 primary derivatives (cuts + hook swaps) + 2-4 secondary (stills, quotes). Compliance check every derivative.

**Phase 2: Influencer Seeding (Month 2-4)**
8. **Build target list**: Map your influencer landscape in 3 tiers — Dream (aspirational), Achievable (Month 6+), Micro (start here).
9. **Vet candidates**: Weighted scorecard (7 criteria, minimum 3.5/5.0). Check red flags. Pre-outreach competitive screen for mid-tier+ targets.
10. **Seed product**: Send to 30-50 nano/micro-influencers. Include compliance package. No cash payment. Track who posts organically.

**Phase 3: Paid Partnerships (Month 3-6)**
11. **Sign hybrid deals**: Small flat fee + affiliate commission. Only after UGC ads prove unit economics (>1.5x ROAS gate).
12. **Execute contracts**: Use contract templates. Legal review required. 90-day content live minimum. FTC disclosure mandatory.

**Phase 4: Scale & Optimize (Month 6+)**
13. **Monthly review**: Pipeline conversion, top/under performers, budget vs results, creative fatigue, competitive moves, content retirement audit.
14. **Scale triggers**: >10 partnerships → consider content coordinator. >$50K/mo revenue → consider influencer platform (Grin, AspireIQ). Bridge top performers to ambassador program.

## Component 2: Grading Rubric

| Grade | Criteria |
|-------|----------|
| **A (9-10)** | Full program running both tracks. Verified ROI data. Attribution framework tracking direct + indirect. Compliance package legally reviewed. 10+ active partnerships. Content retirement policy active. |
| **B (7-8)** | Both tracks active. Scorecard-based vetting. Contract templates in use. Compliance package exists (may not be legally reviewed). Attribution tracking in place. 5-10 partnerships. |
| **C (5-6)** | One track active (creator OR influencer, not both). Basic vetting. Some compliance documentation. Limited attribution tracking. <5 partnerships. |
| **D (3-4)** | Ad hoc partnerships. No formal vetting. No compliance package. No attribution tracking. Inconsistent results. |
| **F (1-2)** | No program. Occasional gifted product with no tracking or strategy. |

## Adaptation Guide

### For Other DTC Supplement Brands
- Use as-is with product-specific customization
- Compliance package is critical — supplement claims are FDA/FTC regulated
- Influencer map framework applies — repopulate with category influencers

### For Non-Supplement DTC Brands
- Remove supplement-specific compliance requirements (FTC health claims)
- Adjust vetting scorecard weights (content quality may matter more than brand alignment)
- Simplify contract templates (no health claim restrictions needed)
- Repurposing system is universal

### For B2B / Service Brands
- Creator/UGC track less applicable — focus on influencer/thought leader track
- Adjust tiers (LinkedIn influencers, industry analysts, podcast hosts)
- Longer deal cycles, higher-touch relationships
- Content repurposing focuses on case studies, testimonials, speaking clips

---

## Universal Principles (Apply to Any Trade)

1. **Creators first, influencers second** — build your ad creative pipeline before paying for audience reach
2. **Vet before you spend** — scorecard-based vetting prevents wasted partnerships
3. **Compliance before seeding** — build the compliance package before anyone touches your product
4. **Track both attribution types** — code-only ROAS undercounts; measure branded search lift too
5. **Phase your budget** — don't pay for influencer deals until you've proven unit economics through ads
6. **Retire content responsibly** — perpetual rights ≠ perpetual usage. Audit semi-annually.
