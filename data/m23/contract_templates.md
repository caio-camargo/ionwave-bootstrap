- We can run this as a paid ad on Facebook, Instagram, TikTok, YouTube, or any platform

- Same restrictions as influencer agreement (see Template A)

- This is a **work-for-hire arrangement**
- Creator retains right to include content in personal portfolio (no commercial use)

**TERMINATION:**
- Brand may terminate if content doesn't meet brief after 2 revision rounds — creator receives 50% kill fee
- Creator may withdraw before filming begins — must return advance payment

## Pre-Launch Compliance Package (U2 — Dialogue Upgrade, GATING REQUIREMENT)

**Before ANY influencer, creator, or product seeding engagement, you MUST have these 4 components ready:**

### Component 1: Approved Talking Points Card
- 1-page document listing exactly what partners CAN say about IonWave
- Structure/function claims only (FDA-compliant)
- Examples: "supports hydration", "contains 78 trace minerals", "sourced from deep-ocean water"
- [VOID — requires: M7 Regulatory workshop for approved claim language]

### Component 2: Prohibited Claims List
- 1-page "NEVER SAY THIS" list shipped with every product and contract
- Disease claims: "cures", "treats", "prevents", "heals"
- Specific outcome claims: "you'll have more energy", "better sleep guaranteed"
- Comparative claims: "better than LMNT", "healthier than Liquid IV"
- Before/after claims without FDA-compliant disclaimer
- [VOID — requires: M7 Regulatory workshop for comprehensive prohibited list]

### Component 3: Content Monitoring Plan
- Weekly scan of tagged/hashtagged posts mentioning IonWave
- Tools: Brand24 or Mention.com ($49-99/mo) OR manual Instagram/TikTok search (free)
- Check every influencer post within 24hrs of going live
- Flag any content making prohibited claims

### Component 4: Claim Violation Response Protocol
1. **Detect**: Monitoring flags a claim violation
2. **Assess**: Is it a minor wording issue or a material health claim?
3. **Contact**: DM/email within 4 hours requesting edit or takedown
4. **Escalate**: If no response in 24hrs, invoke contract termination clause
5. **Document**: Log every incident for legal record
6. **Learn**: Update talking points card if wording was ambiguous

**This package is NON-NEGOTIABLE. Do not seed product, sign contracts, or engage partners until all 4 components exist.** The cost of one FTC enforcement action ($10K+ minimum, plus brand damage) far exceeds the cost of building this package.
