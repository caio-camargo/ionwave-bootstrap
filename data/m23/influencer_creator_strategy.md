## 1. The Core Distinction

Two different models. Same person can play both roles, but the **deal structure, purpose, and economics are different**.

| Dimension | Influencer | Creator (UGC) |
|-----------|-----------|---------------|
| **Primary purpose** | Posts to THEIR audience — renting reach | Creates content for YOU to run as ads — buying assets |
| **Where content lives** | Their social channels | Your ad accounts, website, emails |
| **Who sees it** | Their followers | Your targeting (paid media) |
| **Main value** | Audience trust + credibility | Authentic-looking content at scale |
| **Payment model** | Flat fee + affiliate % (hybrid recommended) | Per video/asset fee |
| **Typical cost** | $50-$10K+ per post (tier-dependent) | $50-$300 per video |
| **Usage rights** | Usually organic only (paid amplification costs extra) | Full ad rights (in perpetuity recommended) |
| **Scalability** | Limited by their audience size | Unlimited with ad spend |
| **Control** | Low — their creative style | High — follows your brief |
| **Best for** | Awareness, credibility, niche targeting | Conversion, ad testing, creative volume |

[Confidence: B | Source: Danilo tabs 500, 501, 508, 509; corroborated by industry standard practice | Date: 2026-02-08]

## 2. When to Use Each

### Use Influencers When:
- Building brand awareness in a specific niche community
- Need credibility/social proof from a trusted voice
- Want to reach a specific audience you can't target with ads alone
- Launching a product and want authentic endorsement
- Have budget for organic reach (minimum viable: $500-2,000/month)

### Use Creators (UGC) When:
- Need high volume of ad creative for testing hooks, angles, formats
- Want authentic-looking ads (not polished studio production)
- Optimizing for conversion (ROAS-driven)
- Need diverse faces/demographics in ads
- Budget is limited — cheaper per asset than influencer posts
- You control the message exactly via briefs

### Combined Strategy (Recommended for IonWave)
Best approach: **Use BOTH strategically**, phased by maturity:

**Phase 1 (Month 1-3): Creator-first**
- 3-5 UGC creators making 10-20 videos/month [Confidence: C | Source: Danilo tab 508 recommendation, scaled down for Founder Mode | Date: 2026-02-08]
- Test hooks, angles, formats via paid ads
- Scale winners with ad spend
- Budget: ~$1,500-3,000/month for content + ad spend separate

**Phase 2 (Month 3-6): Add Influencers**
- Start with product seeding to 20-30 nano/micro-influencers (gifted only)
- Sign 2-3 micro-influencers on hybrid deals ($100-250 flat + 15% affiliate)
- Repurpose influencer content as social proof on site and in emails
- Budget: ~$500-1,500/month for influencer deals + product cost

**Phase 3 (Month 6+): Scale Both**
- 5-10 creators on ongoing contracts
- 5-10 active influencer partnerships
- Move successful nanos/micros to paid hybrid deals
- Test mid-tier influencers ($1K-5K) if proven ROI from micro tier
- Consider influencer-to-creator hybrid: pay for content + usage rights

[Confidence: C | Source: Danilo's combined strategy recommendation (tab 508), adapted for IonWave's Founder Mode phasing | Date: 2026-02-08]

## 4. Deal Structures

### 1. Gifted Only (Product Seeding)
- **What**: Free product, no cash payment, no guaranteed post
- **Cost**: Product COGS only (~$18.85-$37.70 at 40% gross margin on $47.14 unit)
- **Best for**: Nano influencers, initial seeding, building relationships
- **Expected response rate**: ~5-15% will post organically [Confidence: D | Source: Industry benchmark, highly variable | Date: 2026-02-08]
- **IonWave recommendation**: Seed 30-50 nanos in Phase 2. Cost: ~$600-$1,900 in product. Even 5 organic posts = $120-$380 per authentic post.

### 2. Flat Fee
- **What**: Fixed payment per post/deliverable
- **Cost**: Tier-dependent ($50-$5,000+)
- **Best for**: One-off campaigns, guaranteed deliverables
- **Risk**: No performance alignment — they get paid regardless of results

### 3. Affiliate Only
- **What**: Commission on sales (15-30%), no upfront payment
- **Cost**: Variable — only pay on results
- **Best for**: Performance-focused, budget-friendly
- **Risk**: Good influencers won't work on pure affiliate (they know their value)
- **IonWave affiliate rate**: 15-20% recommended. At $47.14 unit price, that's $7.07-$9.43 per sale. With ~40% gross margin ($18.86 gross profit), affiliate commission is 37-50% of gross profit — manageable if CAC < $18 target. [Confidence: B | Source: Cross-referenced with M25 Financial Operations margin data | Date: 2026-02-08]

### 4. Hybrid (RECOMMENDED for IonWave)
- **What**: Smaller flat fee + affiliate percentage
- **Cost**: $100-250 flat + 15% commission (for micro tier)
- **Best for**: Ongoing partnerships, aligns incentives
- **Why it's best**: Influencer gets baseline payment (respects their work), plus upside if it converts. Brand gets performance alignment.
- **IonWave example deal**: $150 flat + 15% affiliate + free product. Break-even at 2 sales through their code. Anything beyond = profit.
  - Math: $150 flat + $18.85 product cost = $168.85 total. Each sale through affiliate: $47.14 revenue - $28.28 COGS - $7.07 affiliate = $11.79 net. Break-even: $150 / $11.79 = ~13 sales. [Confidence: B | Source: Calculated from M25 financial model unit economics | Date: 2026-02-08]

[Confidence: B | Source: Danilo tabs 501, 509; unit economics from M25 | Date: 2026-02-08]

## 5. Founder Mode Phasing

| Phase | Timeline | Focus | Monthly Budget | Cash Type | Key Action |
|-------|----------|-------|---------------|-----------|------------|
| **Phase 0** (Pre-Launch) | Month -2 to 0 | Product seeding only | $0 cash + ~$600-1,900 product COGS | Product cost only | Seed 20-30 nanos with free product. No cash payments. Compliance Package must ship with product. |
| **Phase 1** (Month 1-3) | Post-Launch | **UGC creators** (ad creative) | $1,500-3,000 | Cash (IS the ad creative budget) | 3-5 creators making test content for paid ads. This IS the advertising budget — not separate from it. |
| **Phase 2** (Month 3-6) | Growth | Add paid influencer deals | $2,000-4,000 | Cash (incremental) | 2-3 hybrid deals ($150 flat + 15%). **Gate: only start after UGC ads achieve >1.5x ROAS consistently.** |
| **Phase 3** (Month 6-12) | Scale | Expand both programs | $4,000-8,000 | Cash (incremental) | 5-10 creators, 5-10 influencers. Test mid-tier if micro proves ROI. |
| **Phase 4** (Month 12+) | Mature | Full program | $8,000-15,000+ | Cash (incremental) | Consider macro tier. Ambassador program (→ M22). |

**Key distinction (U3 — Dialogue Upgrade):**
- **Phase 0 is NOT "influencer spend"** — it's product seeding (COGS only). No cash changes hands.
- **Phase 1 creator spend IS the ad creative budget** — you're not choosing between "ads" and "creators." Creators produce the creative that runs in your ads.
- **Phase 2 influencer spend IS incremental** — only start after proving unit economics through UGC ads. **Unit Economics Validation Gate**: UGC ads must achieve >1.5x ROAS for at least 2 consecutive weeks before investing cash in influencer deals.

**Phase 0 compliance note**: Product seeding creates a material connection under FTC guidelines even without cash payment. The Pre-Launch Compliance Package (see contract_templates.md) MUST ship with every seeded product.

[Confidence: B | Source: Phasing adapted from IonWave Founder Mode principles (M0, M17); Phase 0/1 distinction clarified via persona dialogue Round 3; budget estimates are C-grade projections needing validation | Date: 2026-02-08]
