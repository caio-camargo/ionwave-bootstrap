### Pre-Outreach Competitive Screening (U5 — Dialogue Upgrade)

**Before spending ANY time on Tier 2 outreach, complete this 15-minute screen per target:**

| Check | How | Red Flag |
|-------|-----|----------|
| Recent sponsorships | Review last 30 days of posts/stories for #ad, #sponsored tags | Currently promoting a competing electrolyte/mineral brand |
| Competitor affiliate page | Google "[competitor] affiliates/ambassadors" — check if target is listed | Listed as LMNT, AG1, or Liquid IV affiliate |
| Exclusivity status | Look for "ambassador" language in their bio or recent branded content | Active ambassador for a competing brand |
| Brand deal cadence | How many brands do they promote per month? | >3 brand deals/month = low authenticity, audience fatigue |

**Decision**: If any red flag fires, move target to "watch list" and re-screen in 90 days. Don't waste outreach effort on locked targets.

## Tier 3: Micro-Influencer Targets (Phase 2 — START HERE)

Volume > individual reach. 50 micro-influencers > 1 macro. This is where IonWave builds its foundation.

### Target Niches (in priority order for IonWave):

| Niche | Why | Where to Find | Est. Size |
|-------|-----|---------------|-----------|
| **Keto/Carnivore creators** (10-50K) | Electrolyte-aware, understand mineral depletion, high-intent audience | Instagram, TikTok, YouTube | Large (thousands of creators) |
| **CrossFit gym owners & coaches** | Electrolytes are part of their daily routine, trust within their gym community | Instagram, local community | Medium (hundreds) |
| **Nutrition coaches & dietitians** | Credibility, can discuss marine minerals with authority | Instagram, TikTok | Medium |
| **Fasting community leaders** | Electrolytes are critical during fasting, natural product-market fit | YouTube, Facebook groups, Instagram | Medium |
| **Biohacking newsletter writers** | Written content, link-based attribution (easy to track), health-optimizer audience | Substack, personal blogs | Small (dozens) |
| **Endurance athletes** (runners, triathletes, cyclists) | Heavy electrolyte users, understand mineral needs | Instagram, Strava community | Medium |

### Micro-Influencer Approach

**Strategy**: Volume. Free product + affiliate code.
- **Cost per partnership**: $18.85 product cost + $0 cash (gifted) or $100-250 flat (hybrid)
- **Target**: Seed 30-50 in Month 2-3
- **Expected conversion**: 5-15% will post organically; 30-50% will accept affiliate deal with flat fee
- **Tracking**: Unique promo codes per influencer (e.g., IONWAVE_[NAME]15 for 15% off)
