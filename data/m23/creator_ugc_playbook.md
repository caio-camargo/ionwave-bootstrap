## 1. The UGC Creator Model

UGC Creators = people who create content for you to run as ads. They are NOT posting to their audience. You are BUYING their content to use in YOUR ads.

**Why UGC works for IonWave:**
- Authentic feel (not polished studio ads — supplement buyers are skeptical of overproduced content)
- Diverse faces and styles (test which demographics resonate)
- Scalable content production (3-5 creators = 10-20 videos/month)
- Lower cost than traditional production ($50-300/video vs $2,000+ for studio)
- Pairs with M14's testing framework — each UGC video is a creative test

[Confidence: B | Source: Danilo tab 502; industry UGC adoption rates for DTC supplements | Date: 2026-02-08]

## 3. Creator Brief Essentials

**Every UGC brief MUST include these 7 elements:**

### 1. Product Information
- What is IonWave? Marine plasma mineral supplement
- Key benefits: 78 bioavailable minerals from deep-ocean water, not lab-synthesized electrolytes
- Differentiator: Natural ocean-sourced vs. manufactured (LMNT, Liquid IV)
- [VOID — requires: Approved product claim language from M7 Regulatory. DO NOT let creators make health claims without legal review.]

### 2. Target Audience
- Who should this speak to? Specify per video:
  - Health-conscious 28-45, active lifestyle
  - Keto/carnivore community (electrolyte-aware)
  - Athletes/CrossFitters (performance angle)
  - Biohackers (mineral optimization angle)

### 3. Video Specifications
- **Length**: 30-60 seconds (15s for hooks-only cuts)
- **Format**: Vertical 9:16
- **Resolution**: 1080x1920 minimum
- **Delivery**: Raw .mp4 files (no editing — we edit for different cuts)

### 4. Hook Options
- Give creators 2-3 hooks to try per video
- Cross-reference M14 (Testing) hook library when available
- Example hooks for IonWave:
  - Problem: "I used to feel exhausted by 2pm every day..."
  - Curiosity: "I just discovered what's actually in my electrolyte mix..."
  - Social proof: "My nutritionist told me about this..."
  - Contrast: "I switched from [generic electrolyte] and here's what happened..."

### 5. Key Talking Points
- Must mention: [VOID — requires brand-approved talking points from M8]
- Natural ocean-sourced minerals (78 trace minerals)
- Not lab-synthesized
- How to use (mixing, routine)

### 6. Do's and Don'ts

**DO:**
- Be authentic and natural (not scripted-sounding)
- Show the actual product (unboxing, mixing, drinking)
- Share genuine reactions
- Film in natural lighting
- Include captions/text overlay if comfortable

**DON'T:**
- Make specific health claims ("cures", "treats", "prevents" anything)
- Compare to competitors by name (legal risk)
- Use before/after claims without disclaimer
- Film in a bathroom or messy setting
- Over-produce — authenticity > production value

[Confidence: A | Source: FTC guidelines for supplement endorsements; brand safety best practices | Date: 2026-02-08]

### 7. Reference Videos
- Include 2-3 examples of what good UGC looks like
- [VOID — requires: Build a reference library from first 5 approved UGC videos. Until then, use competitor UGC examples found on TikTok/Instagram for LMNT, AG1.]

## 4. Content Repurposing System

**One piece of UGC content should become MANY assets.**

### From a Single 60-Second UGC Video:

| Derivative | Format | Use Case |
|-----------|--------|----------|
| Full 60s video | Video ad | Facebook/Instagram/TikTok ads |
| 30s cut | Video ad | Instagram Reels, TikTok |
| 15s hook-only cut | Video ad | Story ads, pre-roll |
| 3-5 different hooks on same body | Video ad variations | A/B testing hooks (M14 framework) |
| Static screenshots | Image ad | Facebook/Instagram feed |
| Testimonial quotes | Text + image | Website, email social proof |
| Email content | Embedded GIF/image | Post-purchase sequence (M17), welcome series |
| Website content | Embedded video | Product page, testimonials page |
| Retargeting creative | Video ad | Cart abandonment retargeting |

**Multiplier effect (U6 — Dialogue Upgrade, revised)**:
- **Primary derivatives** (reliable quality): 4-6 assets — full video, 30s cut, 15s hook, 2-3 hook swaps on same body. All video, all usable.
- **Secondary derivatives** (quality varies): 2-4 assets — static screenshots, quote pulls, email embeds, website embeds. May or may not be usable depending on source video quality.
- **Realistic total**: 1 UGC video → 6-10 usable assets. At $100-150/video, effective cost per asset = $10-25.

[Confidence: B | Source: Industry repurposing framework, revised downward per persona dialogue Round 6 | Date: 2026-02-08]

### Repurposing Checklist (Per Content Piece)

**Immediate (within 24hrs of approval):**
- [ ] **Compliance check**: Does this content contain any claims NOT in the approved talking points? If yes, flag and do not deploy. (U6 — Dialogue Upgrade)
- [ ] Create 60s, 30s, 15s cuts
- [ ] Test 3-5 different hooks on same body
- [ ] Extract 2-3 stills for static ads
- [ ] Pull quotes for social proof library
- [ ] **Compliance check on derivatives**: Does any derivative (quote, still, cut) contain unapproved claims when taken out of context? If yes, do not use that derivative.

**Within 1 week:**
- [ ] Add to testimonial library (website)
- [ ] Consider for email content (M17 flows)
- [ ] Set up retargeting variant

**For Winners (>2x ROAS target):**
- [ ] Brief 2 new creators with same hook/angle but different person
- [ ] Create variations (different person, same script)
- [ ] Test on additional platforms
- [ ] Add to "winning hooks" reference library for future briefs
- [ ] Increase ad spend on this creative
- [ ] Document what worked (hook type, angle, creator style) in creative log

| Week | Action | Time |
|------|--------|------|
