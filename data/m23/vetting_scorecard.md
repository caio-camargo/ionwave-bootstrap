## 1. Weighted Vetting Scorecard

Score every potential influencer partner before outreach. Minimum score of **3.5/5.0** to proceed with outreach. Score of **4.0+** qualifies for paid partnership.

| # | Criteria | Weight | Score (1-5) | Scoring Guide |
|---|----------|--------|-------------|---------------|
| 1 | **Audience Fit (ICP Match)** | 25% | ___ | 5: Followers are exactly our ICP (health-conscious 28-45, active). 3: Partial overlap. 1: Wrong audience. |
| 2 | **Engagement Rate** | 20% | ___ | 5: >5% engagement. 4: 3-5%. 3: 2-3%. 2: 1-2%. 1: <1% (likely fake). |
| 3 | **Content Quality** | 15% | ___ | 5: Professional, authentic, great aesthetic. 3: Decent quality. 1: Low effort, inconsistent. |
| 4 | **Brand Alignment** | 15% | ___ | 5: Values match, health/wellness focus, no conflicting content. 3: Neutral. 1: Misaligned values or promotes junk. |
| 5 | **Audience Authenticity** | 10% | ___ | 5: Real, organic growth. 3: Some questions. 1: Obvious fake followers/bot engagement. |
| 6 | **Past Brand Results** | 10% | ___ | 5: Proven sales driver for other brands. 3: Some brand work, results unknown. 1: No track record. |
| 7 | **Responsiveness** | 5% | ___ | 5: Quick, professional. 3: Average. 1: Unreliable, ghosts. |

**Weighted Total**: Sum of (Score × Weight) = ___/5.0

**Decision thresholds:**
- **4.0-5.0**: Proceed with paid hybrid deal
- **3.5-3.9**: Proceed with product seeding / affiliate only
- **3.0-3.4**: Pass — not worth the management time
- **Below 3.0**: Hard no

## 2. Pre-Scorecard Vetting Checklist

**Quick pass/fail before investing time in the full scorecard:**

- [ ] Audience alignment — are their followers our ICP?
- [ ] Engagement rate — >3% for micro, >5% for nano?
- [ ] Comment quality — real conversations vs "Nice!" "Love this!" (bot signals)
- [ ] Content quality — would we be proud to be associated?
- [ ] Brand fit — values, aesthetic, tone consistent with IonWave?
- [ ] Past partnerships — do they work with everyone indiscriminately?
- [ ] Authenticity — do they actually use similar products?
- [ ] Growth trend — growing or declining?

**If ANY of these fail → skip full scorecard, move to next candidate.**

## 3. Red Flags (Instant Disqualifiers)

Any single red flag = **automatic rejection**, regardless of scorecard total.

| Red Flag | Why It Matters | How to Check |
|----------|---------------|-------------|
| Engagement <1% with 10K+ followers | Fake followers — you're paying for phantom reach | Social Blade, manual calculation |
| Generic comments only ("Nice!" "Love this!" "🔥🔥🔥") | Bot engagement — no real community | Scroll through 20+ comments on 5 recent posts |
| Promotes everything indiscriminately | No authenticity — their audience tunes out sponsored content | Review last 20 posts for sponsorship frequency |
| Past controversy | Brand safety risk — association damage | Google "[name] controversy", check Twitter |
| Audience is wrong geography | IonWave ships US-only at launch; international followers = wasted reach | HypeAuditor or Modash audience demographics |
| Engagement from wrong demo | Even if followers seem right, engagement from wrong age/interest = poor conversion | Audience analytics tools |
| Huge follower spikes | Bought followers — will show sudden jumps on Social Blade | Social Blade growth chart |
| Promotes direct competitors (LMNT, Liquid IV) actively | Exclusivity conflict, audience already committed elsewhere | Review recent posts and stories |
| Makes unsubstantiated health claims | FTC/FDA liability — IonWave could be implicated | Review their supplement-related content for compliance |

[Confidence: B | Source: Danilo tabs 501, 510 red flags; FTC compliance flag added for supplement-specific risk | Date: 2026-02-08]

## 4. Vetting Tools

| Tool | Purpose | Cost | IonWave Recommendation |
|------|---------|------|----------------------|
| **HypeAuditor** | Fake follower detection, audience quality score | Freemium ($0-$300/mo) | Use free tier for top 10 candidates; paid if scaling to 20+ partners |
| **Social Blade** | Growth pattern analysis, follower trajectory | Free | **Always check** — takes 30 seconds per influencer |
| **Modash** | Audience demographics (age, location, interests) | $120+/mo | Phase 3+ when managing 10+ partnerships |
| **Manual review** | Comment quality, content assessment, brand fit | Free (time) | **Always do** — 10 min per influencer, non-negotiable |

[Confidence: C | Source: Danilo tab 510 tool list; pricing needs verification (may have changed) | Date: 2026-02-08]

## 5. IonWave-Specific Vetting Criteria

Beyond the standard scorecard, IonWave influencers should be vetted for:

### Supplement Category Fit
- [ ] Do they talk about minerals, electrolytes, or hydration?
- [ ] Are they in the keto/carnivore/paleo/biohacking space?

### Compliance Safety
- [ ] Do they make careful health claims (not "this cured my..." language)?
