### **1. Objective**
What is the goal of this ad?
- [ ] Generate purchases (conversion campaign)
- [ ] Drive traffic to landing page
- [ ] Build awareness / brand recall
- [ ] Test new angle/hook

**Primary KPI**: CPA <$30 / ROAS >2.5x / Hook Rate >25%

---

### **2. Target Audience**
Who are we talking to?

**Primary ICP** (from M27 customer_research_icp.md):
- **Wellness Optimizers** (30-50, health-conscious, supplement-savvy, biohacker-lite, $75K+ income)
- **Female Wellness Seekers** (28-45, mothers, wellness influencer followers, budget-conscious but quality-focused)
- **Endurance Athletes** (25-40, runners/cyclists/CrossFit, chasing performance edge, spend $500-1K/year on supplements)

**Key Insight**: Audience is skeptical of generic supplement claims. Craves specificity, transparency, science.

### **3. Angle**
What's the strategic positioning?

**Selected Angle**: [Choose from M12 angle_library.md]
- [ ] Missing Mineral (curiosity gap: "Your electrolytes are missing 75 minerals")
- [ ] Contrarian (problem reframe: "Your sports drink is a scam")
- [ ] Comparison (transparency: "LMNT has 3 minerals, IonWave has 78")
- [ ] Problem Reframe ("You're not dehydrated, you're mineral-depleted")
- [ ] Authority/Science (René Quinton story, marine plasma origins)
- [ ] Testimonial (personal transformation story)

**Why This Angle?** [1 sentence rationale — e.g., "Missing Mineral performed best in Month 1 testing"]

---

### **4. Hook**
What are the first 3 seconds?

**Hook**: [Choose from M12 hook_library.md or write custom]

**Example Hooks**:
- "Your electrolytes are missing the one thing that actually matters"
- "I tested every electrolyte brand. They're all missing the same thing"
- "POV: You just learned Gatorade is 90% sugar"
- "Before I tell you about the best electrolyte I've ever used..."

**Hook Test**: Does the first sentence make you want to keep watching? Yes / No

---

### **5. Key Messages**
What claims/facts must be included?

**Required Claims**:
1. **78 Minerals**: "Marine plasma delivers all 78 minerals your body needs" (vs 3 in LMNT/Gatorade)
2. **Zero Sugar**: "No sugar, no artificial flavors, no BS"
3. **Bioavailability**: "Same mineral ratio as your blood — your cells recognize it" (DO NOT claim "98% bioavailable" without substantiation)
4. **Unique Sourcing**: "Sourced from deep ocean seawater, 30 meters deep"

**Optional Claims** (use if space allows):
- René Quinton story ("Used by French scientist in 1890s to save 50,000 lives")
- Comparison ("LMNT costs $3/serving, IonWave is $1.50")
- Testimonial ("My cramps stopped in 3 days")

**Prohibited Claims** (FTC compliance):
- NO disease claims ("cures", "treats", "prevents diabetes/cancer/etc.")
- NO unsubstantiated claims ("98% bioavailable" without RCT data)
- NO fictional testimonials (real customers only, or clearly marked as actor)

---

### **6. Tone & Style**
How should this sound/feel?

**Brand Voice** (from M8 brand_identity):
- **Authentic**: Real people, real results (not corporate/salesy)
- **Science-Informed**: Credible but accessible (not academic jargon)
- **Confident**: Proud of product, not apologetic
- **Direct**: No fluff, get to the point

**Visual Style**:
- **Raw > Polished**: iPhone-quality OK, studio production NOT required
- **Fast-Paced**: 2-3 second cuts (especially TikTok)
- **Relatable**: Everyday settings (kitchen, gym, car), not glossy studios
- **Text Overlays**: Use bold, large text (e.g., "78 minerals vs 3")

## 4. Brief Checklist (Before Sending)

Before sending brief to creator, verify:

- [ ] Objective is clear (conversion? awareness? testing?)
- [ ] Angle is specific (not vague "make it engaging")
- [ ] Hook is provided or creator is clear on hook framework
- [ ] Key messages are listed (78 minerals, zero sugar, etc.)
- [ ] FTC compliance noted (prohibited claims listed)
- [ ] Style references attached (winner videos, competitor examples)
- [ ] Technical specs match platform (9:16 for TikTok, etc.)
- [ ] Budget and timeline agreed
- [ ] Usage rights specified

**Time to Complete Brief**: 10-20 minutes (using template)
