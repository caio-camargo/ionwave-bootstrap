## OpKit Purpose

**What This OpKit Does**: Provides a complete, reusable system for producing, testing, and scaling paid ad creative for D2C brands (Meta, TikTok, YouTube).

**Who This Is For**:
- Solo founders launching D2C brands ($0-50K MRR)
- Small teams (2-5 people) scaling D2C ads ($50K-500K MRR)
- Agencies managing D2C creative for clients

**What You Get**:
1. **Strategy Frameworks** (creative philosophy, velocity targets, testing mindset)
2. **Production Systems** (replenishment, queue management, naming conventions)
3. **Creative Libraries** (angles, hooks, copy, scripts)
4. **Performance Tracking** (metrics, dashboards, fatigue detection)
5. **Reusable Templates** (briefs, breakdowns, checklists)

**Time to Implement**: 2-4 hours (initial setup), then 4-8 hours/week (ongoing production)

**Expected Outcomes**:
- **Month 1-3**: 4-6 new ads/week, 10-20% winner rate, CAC baseline established
- **Month 4-6**: 7-11 new ads/week, 15-20% winner rate, 20-30% CAC improvement vs baseline
- **Month 7-12**: 11-17 new ads/week, 20-25% winner rate, compound learning accelerates

## Quick Start Guide (4-Week Onboarding)

### Week -1: Setup (2-4 hours)

**Tasks**:
1. Read creative_strategy.md, ad_psychology.md, creative_engineering.md (60 min)
2. Set up production queue (Google Sheets template from creative_production_queue.md) (30 min)
3. Set up performance tracker (Google Sheets template from creative_performance_tracker.md) (30 min)
4. Set up folder structure (Google Drive: /IonWave_Creative/ with subfolders) (15 min)
5. Customize creative_brief_template.md with your brand (30 min)

**Deliverable**: Production infrastructure ready to launch Week 1.

---

### Week 1: Bootstrap Creative Library (4-6 hours)

**Tasks**:
1. Produce 10 Tier 2 ads (5 UGC, 5 founder-led) using angle_library.md and hook_library.md (4-6 hours)
   - Test 5 angles × 2 hook variants = 10 ads
   - Budget: $500-800 (UGC creators $100-150 each)
2. Launch all 10 ads with $20-30 test budget each ($200-300 total ad spend)
3. Add all 10 ads to performance tracker

**Deliverable**: 10 ads testing, backlog established.

---

### Week 2: Identify Winners (2 hours)

**Tasks**:
1. Monday review: Analyze Week 1 performance (CPA, hook rate, ROAS)
2. Identify 1-2 winners (CPA <target, hook rate >25%)
3. Kill underperformers (CPA >2x target)
4. Plan Week 2 production: 3 derivatives of winners + 2 new concepts

**Deliverable**: Winners identified, production plan for Week 2.

---

### Week 3: Iterate Winners (4-6 hours)

**Tasks**:
1. Produce 3 Tier 1 derivatives of Week 1 winners (hook swaps, text overlays) (2-3 hours)
2. Produce 2 Tier 2 new concepts (test new hooks or formats) (2-3 hours)
3. Launch 5 new ads ($100-150 ad spend)
4. Continue monitoring Week 1 winners for fatigue signals

**Deliverable**: 5 new ads, derivative system validated.

---

### Week 4: Scale + Systematize (2 hours)

**Tasks**:
1. Scale winning ads (increase budget 20-30%)
2. Establish weekly production cadence (Monday review, Tuesday-Thursday produce, Friday launch)
3. Conduct first winner breakdown (creative_performance_tracker.md) (30-45 min)
4. Plan Month 2 production roadmap

**Deliverable**: Weekly cadence established, Month 1 complete.

## Grading Rubric (A/B/C/D/F)

### Dimension 1: Creative Velocity

| Grade | Criteria |
|-------|----------|
| **A** | Consistently producing target velocity (4-6 ads/week Month 1-3, 7-11/week Month 4+). Backlog >10 ready-to-test ads. |
| **B** | Mostly hitting velocity targets (3-5 ads/week Month 1-3). Backlog 5-10 ads. |
| **C** | Inconsistent velocity (1-3 ads/week). Backlog <5 ads. Reactive, not proactive. |
| **D** | Rarely producing new creative (<1 ad/week). No backlog. Ad account stale. |
| **F** | No creative production system. Ad-hoc, chaotic, or non-existent. |

---

### Dimension 2: Winner Hit Rate

| Grade | Criteria |
|-------|----------|
| **A** | 20-25% hit rate (Month 4+). Clear patterns identified (angle/hook/format). Compound learning evident. |
| **B** | 15-20% hit rate. Some patterns identified. Improving over time. |
| **C** | 10-15% hit rate. Random testing, no clear patterns. Not improving. |
| **D** | 5-10% hit rate. Shooting blind. Wasting budget on low-probability tests. |
| **F** | <5% hit rate. Fundamental product-market fit or creative execution issue. |

---

### Dimension 3: Performance Tracking Discipline

| Grade | Criteria |
|-------|----------|
| **A** | All ads logged in tracker. Weekly reviews. Fatigue signals detected early. Winners broken down systematically. |
| **B** | Most ads logged. Weekly reviews usually happen. Some winner breakdowns conducted. |
| **C** | Sporadic tracking. Monthly reviews (not weekly). Ad-hoc winner analysis. |
| **D** | Minimal tracking. No systematic reviews. Decisions based on gut, not data. |
| **F** | No tracking. Flying blind. Can't answer "What's working?" |

---

### Dimension 4: Creative System Maturity

| Grade | Criteria |
|-------|----------|
| **A** | Libraries updated quarterly with performance data. Tier 1 angles identified. Derivatives systematically produced. Brand voice evolving appropriately. |
| **B** | Some library updates. Winner derivatives produced. Brand voice mostly consistent. |
| **C** | Libraries static (no updates). Occasional derivatives. Brand voice inconsistent. |
| **D** | No library maintenance. No derivative system. Brand voice chaotic. |
| **F** | No system. Every ad is a random experiment. No learning compound. |

---

### Dimension 5: Operational Excellence

| Grade | Criteria |
|-------|----------|
| **A** | Production queue managed daily. Briefs clear and consistent. Creators deliver on-time, on-spec. FTC compliance rigorous. |
| **B** | Queue managed weekly. Most briefs clear. Some delivery issues (1-2 QA bounces/month). FTC compliance mostly good. |
| **C** | Queue ad-hoc. Briefs inconsistent. Frequent delivery issues (3-5 QA bounces/month). FTC compliance gaps. |
| **D** | No queue. Briefs vague. Constant rework. FTC compliance weak. |
| **F** | Chaos. No briefs, no standards, no process. High FTC violation risk. |
