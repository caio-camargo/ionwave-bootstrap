## 1. What Is an Angle?

### 1.1 Definition

An **angle** is the persuasion approach — the core story you're telling about why someone should buy. It's the "concept" in M14's testing hierarchy (Tier 1, highest-impact variable).

**Angle vs Hook vs Copy**:
- **Angle** = WHY (the persuasion logic: "your electrolytes are incomplete")
- **Hook** = HOW you open (first 3 seconds that earn attention for the angle)
- **Copy** = WHAT you say (specific words, body content, CTA)

One angle generates many hooks. One hook generates many copy variants. This is why angles are tested first — they're the highest-leverage variable.

### 1.2 Angle Selection Criteria

Good angles meet ALL of these:
1. **Rooted in ICP truth** — addresses a real pain, desire, or belief held by the target customer (from M27 VOC)
2. **Differentiating** — separates IonWave from competitors (not generic "electrolytes are important")
3. **Legally defensible** — can be substantiated per FTC requirements (see M11 compliance)
4. **Scalable** — works across multiple formats and platforms (not platform-specific)
5. **Testable** — produces measurable creative variants for performance testing

## 2. IonWave Angle Taxonomy

### 2.1 Primary Angles (Test First — Month 1-2)

#### ANG-01: Marine Plasma Mechanism

| Dimension | Detail |
|-----------|--------|
| **Core Message** | Your body needs 78 trace minerals from the ocean, not 3 synthetic minerals from a lab |
| **Psychological Lever** | Information gap — "I didn't know my electrolytes were incomplete" |
| **Target ICP** | Biohackers, health-optimizers, supplement-educated buyers |
| **Awareness Stage** | Solution-aware (knows about electrolytes, doesn't know about marine plasma) |
| **Competitive Edge** | Direct differentiation from LMNT (sodium-focused) and Gatorade (sugar-based) |
| **M27 VOC Signal** | "I want to know what's actually in my supplements" — transparency demand |
| **FTC Risk** | LOW — mechanism of action claim, not health outcome claim |
| **# Hooks Ready** | 5 VSL (M11) + 5 ad (M12) = 10 |
| **Testing Priority** | HIGHEST — most unique to IonWave |

**Why Test First**: This is IonWave's most differentiated angle. If marine plasma mechanism resonates, it becomes the foundation of all creative. If it doesn't, we pivot to more commodity messaging (ANG-02 or ANG-03).

## 3. Angle Testing Protocol

### 3.1 Priority Sequence (M14 Alignment)

| Month | Angles to Test | Budget Allocation | Goal |
|-------|---------------|-------------------|------|
| 1 | ANG-01 + ANG-02 | 40% each + 20% ANG-03 | Find resonating concept |
| 2 | Winner from Month 1 + ANG-03 | 60% winner + 40% new | Validate across audiences |
| 3 | Top 2 angles + ANG-04 or ANG-05 | 50% proven + 50% expansion | Expand creative portfolio |
| 4+ | Iterate proven angles + test tertiary | 70% iteration + 30% new | Scale winners, explore |

### 3.2 Angle Kill Criteria

An angle is KILLED when:
- 3+ hooks tested under that angle ALL fail Stage 1 (3s view rate <25%)
- Best-performing hook under angle has CPA > 2x target after $200 total spend
- Angle produces 0 winners after testing 10+ hook variants

An angle is VALIDATED when:
- At least 1 hook achieves winner status (ROAS >= target for 7+ days, 10+ purchases)
- Angle produces winning hooks across 2+ platforms
