## 1. Script Architecture Principles

### 1.1 The Universal Ad Structure

Every effective direct response ad follows a 4-part structure, regardless of length or platform:

```
HOOK (0-3s)  →  PROBLEM/AGITATE (3-15s)  →  SOLUTION/PROOF (15-45s)  →  CTA (last 3-5s)
```

The proportions shift by platform:
- **TikTok (15s)**: Hook 1-2s, Problem 3-5s, Solution 5-8s, CTA 2-3s
- **Meta (30s)**: Hook 3s, Problem 7-10s, Solution 12-15s, CTA 3-5s
- **YouTube (60s)**: Hook 5s, Problem 15-20s, Solution 25-30s, CTA 5-10s

[Confidence: A | Source: M11 script_architecture.md, direct response copywriting frameworks (AIDA, PAS, etc.) | Date: 2026-02]

### 1.2 Key Rule: One Ad, One Message

Never try to communicate multiple angles in a single ad. One angle, one hook, one CTA. If you have 5 angles, make 5 ads.

## 2. Script Templates

### 2.1 Template 1: Problem-Agitate-Solution (PAS)

**Best For**: Problem-aware audiences, Meta Feed, direct response
**Duration**: 15-60 seconds
**Format**: UGC testimonial, founder-led, voice-over

```
[HOOK — 3s]
State the problem directly. Name the pain.
"Your electrolytes are 90% sugar and 3 minerals."

[PROBLEM — 5-10s]
Agitate. Make the problem feel urgent and personal.
"That means every time you drink them after a workout, you're getting a sugar spike — not real hydration. You crash harder. You cramp more. And you wonder why."

[SOLUTION — 10-20s]
Introduce IonWave as the answer. Show the mechanism.
"IonWave is different. 78 trace minerals sourced from deep ocean water. No sugar. No synthetic fillers. Just what your body actually needs to hydrate at the cellular level."

[PROOF — 5-10s]
Social proof, authority, or personal result.
"I switched 3 months ago and my recovery time improved dramatically."
[Disclaimer: Individual results may vary]

[CTA — 3-5s]
Clear, single action.
"Try IonWave risk-free. Link in bio." / "Shop now — 30-day money back guarantee."
```

### 2.2 Template 2: AIDA (Attention-Interest-Desire-Action)

**Best For**: Cold audiences, YouTube pre-roll, educational approach
**Duration**: 30-90 seconds
**Format**: Educational, product demo, authority

```
[ATTENTION — 3-5s]
Pattern interrupt or curiosity gap.
"What if everything you know about hydration is wrong?"

[INTEREST — 10-20s]
Reveal surprising information that changes their understanding.
"Most electrolyte brands give you sodium, potassium, and magnesium — just 3 minerals. But your body uses 78 trace minerals for hydration. The ocean has all 78. Your sports drink has 3."

[DESIRE — 10-30s]
Paint the picture of the better outcome + social proof.
"IonWave is liquid marine plasma — harvested from 2,000 feet deep in the Pacific Ocean. It's the closest thing to your own blood plasma. Athletes, biohackers, and nutritionists are making the switch because the difference is night and day."

[ACTION — 3-5s]
Direct CTA with urgency/incentive.
"Get 20% off your first order. Link below."
```

### 2.3 Template 3: Before-After-Bridge (BAB)

**Best For**: Transformation stories, UGC testimonials, Meta/TikTok
**Duration**: 15-45 seconds
**Format**: UGC testimonial, split-screen visual

```
[BEFORE — 5-10s]
Describe the before state (relatable pain).
"6 months ago I was drinking 3 liters of water a day and still felt exhausted. I'd cramp during workouts. Brain fog every afternoon."

[AFTER — 5-10s]
Describe the after state (desired outcome).
"Now I wake up energized. My workouts are longer. The afternoon crash is gone."

[BRIDGE — 10-20s]
How they got from before to after (product as bridge).
"The difference? I stopped chasing hydration with plain water and started replacing the 78 minerals my body was actually missing. IonWave marine plasma electrolytes — no sugar, no synthetic junk, just ocean minerals."
[Disclaimer: Individual results may vary. This is not medical advice.]

[CTA — 3-5s]
"Try it for yourself. 30-day guarantee."
```

### 2.4 Template 4: Us vs Them (Comparison)

**Best For**: Solution-aware audiences, competitive positioning, Meta/YouTube
**Duration**: 15-45 seconds
**Format**: Product comparison, side-by-side, ingredient reveal

```
[HOOK — 3s]
Set up the comparison.
"3 minerals vs 78 minerals. Let's settle this."

[THEM — 5-10s]
Show competitor weaknesses (factual, not disparaging).
"Most electrolyte brands: sodium, potassium, magnesium. Maybe some sugar. Maybe some artificial flavor. That's it."

[US — 10-20s]
Show IonWave advantages.
"IonWave: 78 trace minerals from deep Pacific ocean water. Zero sugar. Zero artificial ingredients. The same mineral profile as human blood plasma."

[PROOF — 5-10s]
Back it up.
"Don't take my word for it — check the label." [Show actual label]

[CTA — 3-5s]
"See the difference for yourself. Link below."
```

### 2.5 Template 5: UGC Unboxing / First Impression

**Best For**: Top-of-funnel, TikTok native, product-awareness
**Duration**: 15-30 seconds
**Format**: UGC, authentic, casual

```
[HOOK — 2-3s]
Casual opening that signals "real person, not ad."
"Okay so this just arrived and I've been dying to try it..."

[UNBOX — 5-10s]
Show product, packaging, first visual impression.
"So this is IonWave — it's marine plasma electrolytes from the deep ocean. Look at this bottle. Okay the label says 78 trace minerals, zero sugar..."

[FIRST USE — 5-10s]
Try the product on camera. Genuine reaction.
"Let me try it... [drinks] ...okay wow, it's actually really clean. No weird aftertaste like the other ones."

[CTA — 3-5s]
Soft sell.
"I'll do a full review after 2 weeks but I'm already impressed. Link in bio if you want to try."
```

### 2.6 Template 6: Educational / Myth-Bust

**Best For**: YouTube, Meta educational content, authority building
**Duration**: 30-90 seconds
**Format**: Talking head (authority figure), voice-over with visuals

```
[HOOK — 3-5s]
Challenge a common belief.
"Drinking more water doesn't fix dehydration. Here's why."

[MYTH — 10-15s]
Explain the common misconception.
"Most people think dehydration = not enough water. So they drink more. But if you're missing the 78 minerals your cells need to actually absorb that water, you're just flushing it through."

[TRUTH — 10-20s]
Replace with correct understanding (supported by IonWave mechanism).
"Real hydration requires electrolytes — and not just sodium. Your cells need a full spectrum of trace minerals to maintain proper osmotic balance. That's exactly what marine plasma provides — 78 minerals in the ratio your body recognizes."

[BRIDGE — 5-10s]
Connect truth to product.
"That's why we created IonWave — sourced from deep ocean water with the complete mineral profile."

[CTA — 3-5s]
"Learn more at ionwave.com"
```

## 3. Template Selection Guide

| Scenario | Best Template | Why |
|----------|--------------|-----|
| First test of new angle | PAS (#1) | Simple, direct, easy to produce |
| Cold audience, need to educate | AIDA (#2) or Educational (#6) | Builds understanding before selling |
| Have real testimonials | BAB (#3) | Authentic transformation stories sell |
| Competitive positioning | Us vs Them (#4) | Direct comparison when audience is solution-aware |
| TikTok native content | Unboxing (#5) | Platform-native format, feels organic |
| YouTube pre-roll | AIDA (#2) | Needs to earn attention in 5s, then educate |
| Retargeting (warm audiences) | BAB (#3) or Us vs Them (#4) | They know the brand, need final nudge |
| Founder-led content | Any template with founder on camera | Trust + authenticity signal |

## 5. Compliance Overlays (All Templates)

Every script MUST include:

1. **FTC Disclaimer** (if testimonial/result claims): "Individual results may vary. Not typical outcomes."
2. **Structure/Function Language**: Use "supports," "helps maintain," "promotes" — never "cures," "treats," "prevents"
3. **Supplement Disclaimer**: "These statements have not been evaluated by the FDA. This product is not intended to diagnose, treat, cure, or prevent any disease."
4. **Influencer Disclosure** (if UGC/creator): "#ad" or "#sponsored" visible in first 3 seconds
