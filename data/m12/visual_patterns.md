## 2. Pattern Deep Dives

### 2.1 UGC Talking Head

**Why it works**: Faces stop scrolls. Authentic, personal delivery builds trust. Platform algorithms favor face-forward content (higher completion rates).

**Specification**:
- **Framing**: Head + shoulders, centered, good lighting (natural or ring light)
- **Background**: Clean but not sterile (home, kitchen, gym — not studio)
- **Eye Contact**: Direct to camera (not to side)
- **Energy**: Conversational, not scripted-sounding
- **Audio**: Clear voice, minimal background noise
- **Duration**: 15-30s optimal

**IonWave Application**:
- Creator holds IonWave bottle while talking
- Opens with hook, demonstrates product, delivers result
- Works with Templates 1 (PAS), 3 (BAB), 5 (Unboxing) from script_templates.md

**Production Tips**:
- Shoot 3-5 takes — the third is usually the most natural
- Leave "ums" and natural pauses in — too polished = too "ad-like"
- Vertical orientation (9:16) for TikTok/Reels, square (1:1) crop for Meta Feed

[Confidence: A | Source: UGC performance data from Billo, Insense, JungleTopp platforms; Meta/TikTok creator best practices | Date: 2026-02]

### 2.2 Product Demo

**Why it works**: Visual differentiation — shows what makes IonWave different from competitors.

**Specification**:
- **Close-up shots**: Liquid color, texture, dissolving in water
- **Label shots**: Show ingredient list (78 minerals claim)
- **Pour shots**: Ocean-blue liquid into glass (visual distinctiveness)
- **Comparison shots**: IonWave bottle next to competitor bottles (optional)

**IonWave-Specific Shots** (marine plasma visual assets):
1. **The Pour**: Ocean-blue liquid poured from glass bottle — premium, distinctive
2. **The Dissolve**: If applicable, mineral concentrate dispersing in water — hypnotic visual
3. **The Label Read**: Close-up of "78 trace minerals" claim on label
4. **The Side-by-Side**: IonWave ingredient list vs competitor (3 minerals vs 78)
5. **The Ocean Connection**: Product shot with ocean background (origin story visual)

**Production Tips**:
- Good lighting is critical for product shots (natural daylight or soft box)
- Slow-motion pour shots add premium feel
- Macro lens or close-up mode for ingredient label readability

### 2.3 Before/After Split

**Why it works**: Transformation is the most powerful visual proof. Shows outcome, not just promise.

**Specification**:
- **Split screen**: Left = before state, Right = after state (or sequential)
- **Same person**: Must be same individual for credibility
- **Visual clarity**: Clear, obvious difference
- **Time callout**: "After 2 weeks" — sets expectation

**IonWave Application**:
- **Energy levels**: Sluggish morning (before) → energized morning (after)
- **Workout performance**: Mid-workout cramp (before) → full workout completion (after)
- **Ingredient comparison**: Competitor label (3 minerals) → IonWave label (78 minerals)

**FTC CRITICAL**: Health-related before/after comparisons for supplements are heavily scrutinized. Must include disclaimer: "Individual results may vary. Not intended to diagnose, treat, cure, or prevent any disease." Cannot imply typical results unless substantiated by clinical evidence.

[Confidence: A | Source: FTC Health Claims Enforcement Actions 2023-2024, M11 compliance analysis | Date: 2026-02]

### 2.4 Founder-Led

**Why it works**: Authenticity signal. Founder passion is unscriptable. Builds brand trust disproportionately to cost ($0).

**Specification**:
- **Setting**: Office, kitchen, outdoors — not studio (authenticity)
- **Energy**: Passionate, conversational, personal
- **Script**: Semi-structured (key points, but not word-for-word)
- **Props**: Product always visible
- **Duration**: 30-60s (can be longer on YouTube)

**IonWave Application**:
- Founder explains WHY marine plasma (origin story)
- Founder holds competitor product and IonWave side by side
- Founder at the ocean (sourcing story)

**Critical success factor**: Founder must be comfortable on camera. If not, invest in 1-2 sessions with a video coach. Awkward founder content hurts more than it helps.

### 2.5 Text-on-Screen

**Why it works**: Catches attention in muted autoplay environments. Bold text creates pattern interrupt. Fastest to produce (minutes, not hours).

**Specification**:
- **Font**: Bold, sans-serif, high contrast (white on dark, yellow on dark)
- **Size**: 30-40% of screen height (readable on mobile)
- **Animation**: Typewriter effect, fade-in, or slam-cut (adds energy)
- **B-roll**: Behind text — product shots, lifestyle footage, ocean footage
- **Duration**: 15s optimal (fast pace, 2-3 text cards)

**IonWave Application**:
- Hook card: "YOUR ELECTROLYTES HAVE 3 MINERALS" (bold, alarming)
- Problem card: "Your body needs 78." (smaller, contrasting)
- Solution card: "IonWave. From the ocean. All 78." (brand reveal)
- CTA card: "Try it risk-free" (with product shot behind)

**Production Tools**: Canva, CapCut, InShot — no video editing experience required.

### 2.6 Ingredient Reveal

**Why it works**: Transparency sells. Educated buyers want to see what's in the product.

**Specification**:
- **Close-up label read**: Camera slowly zooms into ingredient panel
- **Highlight key ingredients**: Circle or underline the differentiators
- **Comparison format**: Show 2 labels side by side
- **Narration**: Voice explaining what each ingredient does

**IonWave Application**:
- Show Gatorade/Liquid IV label (3-5 minerals) → IonWave label (78 minerals)
- Highlight "zero sugar, zero artificial ingredients" on IonWave label
- Science explainer: what trace minerals do at the cellular level

### 2.7 Lifestyle Integration

**Why it works**: Shows product in context of aspirational daily routine. Builds desire through association.

**Specification**:
- **Morning routine**: Wake up → IonWave → workout → productive day
- **Gym scene**: Pre/post workout with IonWave
- **Office/desk**: Afternoon energy — IonWave instead of coffee
- **Outdoor**: Running, hiking, yoga with IonWave visible

**IonWave Application**: Marine plasma + active lifestyle = aspirational brand positioning.

### 2.8 Reaction/Unboxing

**Why it works**: Authentic first impressions generate trust and curiosity. Platform-native on TikTok.

**Specification**:
- **Real reaction**: Genuine surprise/delight (not scripted)
- **Product focus**: Packaging quality, first visual impression, first taste
- **Casual setting**: Home, desk — "just received this" energy
- **Duration**: 15-30s (TikTok), 30-60s (Meta/YouTube)

## 4. Visual Production Checklist (Per Shoot)

- [ ] Shot list prepared (minimum 10 shots per concept)
- [ ] Lighting confirmed (natural daylight or ring light)
- [ ] Audio quality tested (lavalier mic for UGC, built-in for casual TikTok)
- [ ] Product clean and well-lit (no fingerprints, label facing camera)
- [ ] 9:16 vertical as primary orientation (can crop to 1:1 and 16:9 in post)
- [ ] Multiple takes per shot (minimum 3)
- [ ] B-roll captured (product close-ups, lifestyle shots, ingredient labels)
- [ ] FTC disclaimers included in visual overlay or caption
- [ ] File naming per creative_naming_convention.md
