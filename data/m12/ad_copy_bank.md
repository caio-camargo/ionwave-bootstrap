## 1. Copy Bank Organization

All copy organized by:
1. **Angle** (e.g., Missing Mineral, Contrarian, Comparison)
2. **Platform** (Meta, TikTok, YouTube)
3. **Length** (Short: <50 words, Medium: 50-100 words, Long: 100-150 words)

### 1.2 Usage Protocol

- **Month 1-2**: Use Angle 1-3 (Missing Mineral, Contrarian, Comparison) — highest expected performance
- **Month 3-4**: Expand to Angle 4-5 (Problem Reframe, Authority)

## 9. Advanced Copy Techniques

### 9.1 Pattern Interrupts

**Technique**: Start with something unexpected to break scroll.

**Examples**:
- "I drink seawater every morning. Let me explain."
- "Your electrolytes are a scam. Here's proof."
- "I threw away $500 worth of LMNT. No regrets."

### 9.2 The "But" Flip

**Technique**: Acknowledge objection, then flip it.

**Examples**:
- "Marine plasma sounds weird. But it's literally what your blood is made of."
- "IonWave costs less than LMNT. But it has 25x more minerals."
- "You've never heard of marine plasma. But René Quinton saved 50,000 lives with it."

### 9.3 Specificity Stacking

**Technique**: Layer specific details to build credibility.

**Examples**:
- "78 minerals. 30 meters deep. Atlantic Ocean. Lab-tested. Zero additives."
- "I tested 47 electrolyte brands over 6 months. Spent $500. Only one worked."

[Confidence: A | Source: David Ogilvy copywriting principles, Eugene Schwartz "Breakthrough Advertising" | Date: 2026-02]

## 10. Copy Testing Protocol

### 10.1 How to Test Copy Variants

**Setup**:
- Same creative visual/video
- 3-5 different copy variations
- $20-30 spend per variant
- Run for 2-3 days

**Metrics**:
- **Primary**: CPA (Cost Per Acquisition)
- **Secondary**: CTR (Click-Through Rate), Hook Rate (for video)

**Decision Rule**:
- **Winner**: Lowest CPA with ≥10 purchases
- **Kill**: CPA >2x target after $50 spend

### 10.2 Copy Iteration Playbook

**Month 1**: Test 5 angle variants (Missing Mineral, Contrarian, Comparison, Problem Reframe, Authority)

**Month 2**: Double down on winning angle, test 5 hook variants within that angle

**Month 3**: Test 3 CTA variants on best-performing hook+angle combo

**Month 4+**: Rotate through angles to combat fatigue

[Confidence: A | Source: M14 testing protocol, M13 first_100_ads.md | Date: 2026-02]

## 11. Copy Compliance Checklist

Before publishing any ad copy, verify:

- [ ] No disease claims ("cures", "treats", "prevents")
- [ ] No unsubstantiated health claims ("98% bioavailable" requires RCT)
- [ ] No fake urgency (only use scarcity if true)
- [ ] No fake testimonials (real customers only)
- [ ] Material connection disclosed (if influencer/paid)
- [ ] Atypical results disclosed ("Results not typical")
- [ ] No before/after images (Meta policy for supplements)

[Confidence: A | Source: FTC Advertising Guidelines, Meta/Google/TikTok ad policies | Date: 2026-02]
