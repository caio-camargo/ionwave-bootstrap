# UGC Creator Production Guide — M12: Ad Creative

**Version**: 1.0.0
**Audience**: UGC Creators (Billo, Insense, Trend, freelance)
**Purpose**: Simple, visual guide for delivering technically correct video files

---

## 1. iPhone Filming Settings (Most Common)

### Step 1: Open Camera App
- Tap **"Video"** mode (NOT photo)
- Select **"4K at 30 fps"** OR **"1080p HD at 30 fps"**
  - **4K** = higher quality, larger file (use if brief says "Best Quality")
  - **1080p** = good quality, smaller file (default for most ads)

### Step 2: Choose Vertical or Square
- **Vertical (9:16)**: Hold phone vertically (portrait) — for TikTok, Instagram Reels, Meta Stories
- **Square (1:1)**: NOT native to iPhone — film vertical, crop in editing
- **Horizontal (16:9)**: Hold phone horizontally (landscape) — ONLY for YouTube

**IonWave Default**: Film VERTICAL (9:16) for 90% of ads.

### Step 3: Filming Tips
- ✅ Natural lighting (near window, outdoors)
- ✅ Steady hands (use phone tripod or prop against object)
- ✅ Clean background (kitchen, living room, NOT messy bedroom)
- ✅ Record 10-20 seconds EXTRA (we'll trim in editing)
- ❌ Don't zoom (degrades quality)
- ❌ Don't film in low light (grainy footage)

## 2. Android Filming Settings

### Step 1: Open Camera App
- Tap **"Video"** mode
- Go to Settings (gear icon)
- Select **"FHD (1920×1080)"** or **"4K"**
- Select **"30 FPS"**

### Step 2: Film Vertical (9:16)
- Hold phone vertically
- Follow same filming tips as iPhone (lighting, steady, clean background)

---

## 3. Editing in CapCut (Free, Easy)

### Step 1: Import Footage
- Open CapCut app (download from App Store / Google Play if needed)
- Tap **"New Project"**
- Select your video clips

### Step 2: Set Aspect Ratio
- Tap **"Format"** (top toolbar)
- Choose:
  - **9:16** (vertical) — for TikTok, Reels, Stories
  - **1:1** (square) — for Meta Feed (if brief specifies)
  - **16:9** (horizontal) — for YouTube (rare)

**IonWave Default**: Use 9:16 unless brief says otherwise.

### Step 3: Edit Video
- Trim to desired length (15s, 30s, 60s)
- Add text overlays (if brief requires):
  - Tap **"Text"** → Type message (e.g., "78 minerals vs 3")
  - Make text LARGE (36pt+ font size)
  - Place at bottom-center or top-center (visible on mobile)
- Add captions (optional but recommended):
  - Tap **"Captions"** → Auto-generate → Review for accuracy

### Step 4: Export
- Tap **"Export"** (top right)
- **Resolution**: **1080p** (default — DO NOT select 720p or 4K unless brief specifies)
- **Frame Rate**: **30 FPS**
- **Quality**: **Best** (or High if file size is an issue)
- Save to phone

## 4. File Check Before Delivery

### Checklist (CRITICAL — Review Every Time)

- [ ] **Resolution**: 1080×1920 (9:16) OR 1080×1080 (1:1) OR 1920×1080 (16:9)
  - **How to Check**: On iPhone, open video → tap Info icon → check dimensions
- [ ] **File Size**: <100MB
  - **How to Check**: Long-press video file → tap "Get Info" (iOS) or "Details" (Android)
  - **If >100MB**: Re-export in CapCut with "High" quality instead of "Best"
- [ ] **Format**: MP4 (default for CapCut/iPhone)
- [ ] **Plays on Phone**: Watch full video to ensure no glitches, freezes, or audio sync issues
- [ ] **Length**: Matches brief (15s / 30s / 60s ± 2 seconds)

**Common Issues**:
- ❌ **720p resolution** (too low) → Re-export in 1080p
- ❌ **File >100MB** (too large) → Compress or re-export with lower quality
- ❌ **Wrong aspect ratio** (filmed horizontal, brief asked for vertical) → Crop in CapCut
- ❌ **Audio out of sync** → Re-edit in CapCut, ensure video+audio tracks aligned

## 5. Delivery

### Step 1: Upload to Cloud
- **Google Drive** (preferred): Upload video → Share link → Set to "Anyone with link can view"
- **Dropbox**: Upload → Share link
- **WeTransfer** (for large files): Upload → Enter recipient email

### Step 2: Deliver Raw Footage + Final Edit
- **Final Edit**: Your edited video (MP4, correct specs)

## 6. Common Mistakes (And How to Avoid)

| Mistake | Why It's a Problem | Fix |
|---------|-------------------|-----|
| **Filmed horizontal instead of vertical** | Wrong aspect ratio, doesn't fit TikTok/Reels | Film vertical OR crop to 9:16 in CapCut |
| **Text overlay too small** | Unreadable on mobile | Use 36pt+ font size, bold style |
| **Audio too quiet** | Viewers can't hear | Speak louder OR boost audio in CapCut (Volume +20%) |
| **File exported in 720p** | Low quality, pixelated on larger screens | Re-export in 1080p (CapCut → Export Settings → Resolution → 1080p) |
| **No captions / text overlays** | Brief said "add text overlay: 78 vs 3" | Add text in CapCut before exporting |
| **File >100MB** | Upload takes forever, some platforms reject | Compress in CapCut (Quality: High instead of Best) |

## 7. Troubleshooting

### Problem: "My file is 150MB, how do I make it smaller?"

**Solution**:
1. Re-export in CapCut
2. Change Quality from "Best" → "High"
3. OR reduce length (trim 5-10 seconds)

---

### Problem: "The operator said my video is 720p, but I filmed in 1080p!"

**Solution**:
- Check CapCut export settings: Resolution should be 1080p, not 720p
- Re-export with correct resolution

---

### Problem: "I don't have CapCut, can I use [Other App]?"

**Solution**:
- **Yes**, but ensure final file meets specs:
  - 1080×1920 (9:16) or 1080×1080 (1:1)
  - MP4 format
  - <100MB
  - 30 FPS
- Popular alternatives: Premiere Rush (paid), iMovie (iOS only), InShot (mobile)
