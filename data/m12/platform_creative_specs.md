## 1. Meta (Facebook & Instagram)

### 1.1 Video Specs

| Spec | Requirement | Recommendation |
|------|------------|----------------|
| **Aspect Ratio** | 1:1 (square), 4:5 (vertical), 9:16 (Stories/Reels) | 9:16 for Reels (80% of placements), 4:5 for Feed |
| **Resolution** | Min 1080×1080 (1:1), 1080×1350 (4:5), 1080×1920 (9:16) | Use min resolution or higher |
| **File Size** | Max 4GB | Keep <100MB for faster upload/processing |
| **Length** | 1 second - 241 minutes | 15-30s (optimal engagement) |
| **Format** | MP4, MOV | MP4 (better compatibility) |
| **Frame Rate** | 23-60 FPS | 30 FPS (standard) |
| **Codec** | H.264, VP8 | H.264 |
| **Audio** | Optional but recommended | AAC, 128kbps+ |

[Confidence: A | Source: Meta Ads Specs 2024 | Date: 2026-02]

---

### 1.2 Image Specs

| Spec | Requirement | Recommendation |
|------|------------|----------------|
| **Aspect Ratio** | 1.91:1 to 1:1 | 1:1 (square) for Feed, 4:5 for Feed (more vertical space) |
| **Resolution** | Min 1080×1080 | 1200×1200 or higher |
| **File Size** | Max 30MB | Keep <5MB |
| **Format** | JPG, PNG | JPG (smaller file size) |

---

### 1.3 Text Overlay

**Meta Policy** (2024):
- **No 20% text rule anymore** (removed 2020)
- Text overlays allowed, but avoid text-heavy images (algorithm may reduce reach)
- Best practice: <20% of image covered by text

**IonWave Application**: Text overlays (e.g., "78 minerals vs 3") are OK, but keep concise.

[Confidence: A | Source: Meta text overlay policy update | Date: 2026-02]

---

### 1.4 Primary Text (Caption)

- **Character Limit**: 125 characters displayed, 2,200 max (truncated with "See More")
- **Best Practice**: Front-load hook in first 125 characters
- **Emoji Use**: OK, but don't overuse (1-2 max)

**Example**:
"Your electrolytes are missing 75 minerals. LMNT, Liquid IV, Gatorade — they all give you 3. Marine plasma has all 78. [See More]"

---

### 1.5 Headline & Description (Link Ads)

- **Headline**: 40 characters max (appears below image/video)
- **Description**: 30 characters max (appears below headline on some placements)
- **Link**: URL (shortened URLs OK)

**Example**:
- Headline: "78 Minerals. Zero Sugar. Try IonWave"
- Description: "The electrolyte with all 78 minerals"

---

### 1.6 Placement Optimization

**Recommended Placements** (Meta Month 1-3):
- **Facebook Feed**: Yes
- **Instagram Feed**: Yes
- **Instagram Reels**: Yes (prioritize)
- **Instagram Stories**: Yes
- **Facebook Stories**: Yes
- **Audience Network**: No (lower quality traffic)
- **Messenger**: No (low engagement for D2C)

**Advantage+ Placements**: Use for broad reach, but monitor if specific placements underperform.

[Confidence: A | Source: M13 account_structure.md, Meta placement best practices | Date: 2026-02]

## 2. TikTok

### 2.1 Video Specs

| Spec | Requirement | Recommendation |
|------|------------|----------------|
| **Aspect Ratio** | 9:16 (vertical), 1:1 (square), 16:9 (horizontal) | 9:16 ONLY (native TikTok format) |
| **Resolution** | Min 540×960 (9:16), 640×640 (1:1) | 1080×1920 (9:16) for best quality |
| **File Size** | Max 500MB | Keep <100MB |
| **Length** | 5-60 seconds | 15-30s (optimal) |
| **Format** | MP4, MOV, MPEG, AVI, WebM | MP4 |
| **Frame Rate** | 23-60 FPS | 30 FPS |
| **Codec** | H.264, H.265 | H.264 |
| **Audio** | Required | AAC, 128kbps+ |

[Confidence: A | Source: TikTok Ads Specs 2024 | Date: 2026-02]

---

### 2.2 TikTok-Specific Best Practices

**Creative Style**:
- **Raw > Polished**: UGC aesthetic outperforms studio production
- **Fast Pace**: First 1 second is make-or-break (faster than Meta's 3-second rule)
- **Trending Audio**: Use trending sounds (increases distribution)
- **Text Overlays**: Large, bold text (easier to read on mobile)
- **Vertical Framing**: Fill the screen, no black bars

**Example**: iPhone-shot video, creator talking directly to camera, trending audio in background, text overlay "78 minerals vs 3", fast cuts every 2-3 seconds.

[Confidence: A | Source: TikTok Creative Best Practices 2024 | Date: 2026-02]

---

### 2.3 Caption & Hashtags

- **Caption Length**: 2,200 characters max (but keep <100 for readability)
- **Hashtags**: 3-5 hashtags (mix of broad + niche)
- **CTA**: "Link in bio" or "Shop now" (depending on ad format)

**Example**:
"POV: you just learned your sports drink is a scam 💀 78 minerals > 3 minerals. Link in bio — IonWave #electrolytes #hydration #biohacking #fitnesstips #supplement"

## 3. YouTube

### 3.1 Video Specs (Skippable In-Stream Ads)

| Spec | Requirement | Recommendation |
|------|------------|----------------|
| **Aspect Ratio** | 16:9 (horizontal), 9:16 (vertical Shorts), 1:1 (square) | 16:9 for standard YouTube, 9:16 for Shorts |
| **Resolution** | Min 640×360 | 1920×1080 (1080p) for best quality |
| **File Size** | Max 256GB | Keep <1GB |
| **Length** | 12 seconds - 6 hours | 60-90s (skippable at 5s, so hook hard) |
| **Format** | Most video formats | MP4 (H.264) |
| **Frame Rate** | 24-60 FPS | 30 FPS |

[Confidence: A | Source: YouTube Ads Specs 2024 | Date: 2026-02]

---

### 3.2 YouTube-Specific Best Practices

**Creative Style**:
- **Hook in First 5 Seconds**: Users can skip at 5s — hook MUST be strong
- **Educational Angle**: YouTube users are in "learning mode" — teach something
- **Higher Production Value**: Users expect more polish than TikTok
- **Longer Format**: 60-90s ads work (vs 15-30s on Meta/TikTok)

**Example**: 60s ad with strong 5s hook ("Before I tell you about the best electrolyte I've ever used..."), educational body (René Quinton story, 78 minerals explanation), direct CTA ("Click below to try IonWave").

[Confidence: A | Source: YouTube Ads Best Practices 2024 | Date: 2026-02]

---

### 3.3 Companion Banner (Optional)

- **Size**: 300×60 pixels

- **Use Case**: Appears next to video ad on desktop
- **IonWave**: Optional (focus on video creative first)

## 5. Cross-Platform Adaptation Strategy

### 5.1 One Creative → Three Platform Variants

**Starting Point**: Shoot one 30-60s vertical (9:16) UGC video.

**Adaptations**:
1. **Meta**: 15-30s cut, add text overlay, hook in first 3 seconds
2. **TikTok**: 15s cut, trending audio overlay, fast pace, large text
3. **YouTube**: 60-90s version (keep full length), add 5-second hook intro

**Why?** One shoot → three platform-optimized ads. Efficient use of production budget.

[Confidence: A | Source: Multi-platform creative workflows | Date: 2026-02]

---

### 5.2 Platform-Specific Do's and Don'ts

| Do's | Don'ts |
|------|--------|
| **Meta**: Test multiple hook variants, use captions for sound-off viewing | Don't use trending TikTok audio (doesn't work on Meta) |
| **TikTok**: Use trending audio, embrace raw UGC aesthetic | Don't cross-post polished Meta ads (low engagement) |
| **YouTube**: Invest in higher production value, longer educational format | Don't use 15s TikTok-style ads (too short for YouTube) |

## 6. Mobile-First Design Principles

### 6.1 Why Mobile-First?

**Stats**:
- 94% of Meta ad impressions are mobile (Meta 2024)
- 100% of TikTok traffic is mobile
- 70%+ of YouTube views are mobile

**Implication**: Design for mobile FIRST, desktop second.

[Confidence: A | Source: Platform usage data 2024 | Date: 2026-02]

---

### 6.2 Mobile Optimization Checklist

- [ ] **Vertical or Square Format**: 9:16 or 1:1 (not 16:9 horizontal)
- [ ] **Large Text**: Readable on small screens (min 24pt font)
- [ ] **Sound-Off Friendly**: Captions or text overlays (80% watch without sound)
- [ ] **Fast Pace**: 2-3 second cuts (mobile users scroll fast)
- [ ] **Clear CTA**: Large, tappable button or text ("Swipe Up", "Link in Bio")

## 7. File Export Settings (For Editors)

### 7.1 Recommended Export Settings (CapCut, Premiere, Final Cut)

**For Meta/TikTok/YouTube**:
- **Format**: MP4
- **Codec**: H.264
- **Resolution**: 1080×1920 (9:16) or 1080×1080 (1:1)
- **Frame Rate**: 30 FPS
- **Bitrate**: 5-10 Mbps (VBR, 2-pass)
- **Audio**: AAC, 128 kbps, 48kHz

**Why These Settings?** Balance between quality and file size. Fast upload, smooth playback.

[Confidence: A | Source: Video export best practices | Date: 2026-02]

---

### 7.2 Compression Tools (If File Too Large)

- **HandBrake** (free): Re-encode to reduce file size
- **Cloudconvert** (free tier): Online compression
- **Adobe Media Encoder** (if you have Adobe Suite)

**Target**: Keep video files <100MB for fast uploads and processing.

## 8. Accessibility (Optional but Recommended)

### 8.1 Captions/Subtitles

**Why?** 80% of users watch video ads without sound (Digiday 2023).

**How to Add**:
- **Auto-Generated**: Meta/TikTok/YouTube auto-generate (review for accuracy)
- **Manual**: Add via editing tool (CapCut, Premiere) or platform
- **Burned-In**: Text overlay in video itself (best for guaranteed visibility)

**IonWave Recommendation**: Burned-in text overlays for key claims (e.g., "78 minerals vs 3").

[Confidence: A | Source: Video accessibility best practices | Date: 2026-02]

---

### 8.2 Alt Text (Images)

**What**: Text description of image for screen readers (accessibility feature).

**Meta/TikTok**: Optional field when uploading image.

**IonWave Example**: "IonWave marine plasma electrolyte sachet next to ocean waves background, text overlay: 78 minerals, zero sugar"

## 9. Platform-Specific Policies (Supplement Ads)

### 9.1 Meta

- **No before/after images** for supplement ads
- **No targeting based on health conditions** (e.g., can't target "diabetes")
- **Health claims must be substantiated** (FTC rules)
- **No exaggerated results** ("Lose 20 lbs in 2 weeks")

[Confidence: A | Source: Meta Ads Policy — Health & Wellness | Date: 2026-02]

---

### 9.2 TikTok

- **Supplement ads restricted** in some categories (weight loss especially)
- **No aggressive health claims**
- **Age targeting**: 18+ only for supplement ads

[Confidence: A | Source: TikTok Ads Policy | Date: 2026-02]

---

### 9.3 YouTube/Google

- **Supplements must be certified** via LegitScript or similar
- **No disease claims** ("cures", "treats")
- **No misleading claims**

[Confidence: A | Source: Google Ads Policy — Healthcare | Date: 2026-02]
