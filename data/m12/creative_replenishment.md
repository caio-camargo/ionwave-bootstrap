## 1. The Creative Replenishment Problem

### 1.1 Why Creative Drought Happens

**Symptom**: Ad account needs new creative, but production pipeline is empty.

**Root Causes**:
1. **Reactive Production**: Only produce when current ads fail (too late)
2. **No Backlog**: Zero ready-to-test assets in reserve
3. **Bottleneck Dependency**: All creative flows through one person/agency
4. **No System**: Ad-hoc requests, no repeatable workflow

**Impact**: CAC spikes 40-80% when fresh creative isn't available to replace fatigued ads (M13 research).

[Confidence: A | Source: M13 roas_decay_model.md, agency benchmarks on creative fatigue | Date: 2026-02]

### 1.2 The Replenishment Target

**Goal**: Always have 10+ ready-to-test ad variants in backlog.

**Why 10+?**
- 5-7 ads fail for every 1 winner (M14 testing protocol)
- Creative fatigue occurs every 2-4 weeks (M13 research)
- Need continuous testing without production delays

[Confidence: A | Source: M14 creative_testing_protocol.md, M13 creative fatigue timelines | Date: 2026-02]

## 2. The 3-Tier Replenishment System

### 2.1 Tier 1: Rapid Derivatives (Same-Day Production)

**Definition**: Low-effort creative refreshes that can be produced in <4 hours.

**Examples**:
- Swap hook (first 3 seconds of video)
- Swap text overlay
- Swap thumbnail/static image
- Swap CTA
- Cut-down (60s → 15s)
- Aspect ratio swap (16:9 → 9:16)

**Production Time**: 1-4 hours
**Cost**: $0-50 (in-house editing tools)
**Hit Rate**: 25-40% (M13 winning_ad_iterations.md)

**When to Use**: Combat creative fatigue on winning ads (refresh every 2-4 weeks).

[Confidence: A | Source: M13 winning_ad_iterations.md | Date: 2026-02]

---

### 2.2 Tier 2: New Concepts (1-3 Day Production)

**Definition**: Net-new ad creative (new script, new footage, new angle).

**Examples**:
- UGC creator video (new script, same product)
- Founder-led testimonial (new story)
- Product demo (new angle)
- Educational explainer (new hook)

**Production Time**: 1-3 days (scripting + filming + editing)
**Cost**: $50-300 (UGC creator fee + editing)
**Hit Rate**: 5-15% (M14 testing protocol)

**When to Use**: Expand winning angle (test new hooks within same concept) or explore new angles.

[Confidence: A | Source: M14 creative_testing_protocol.md | Date: 2026-02]

---

### 2.3 Tier 3: High-Production Assets (1-2 Week Production)

**Definition**: Polished, high-effort creative (studio shoot, professional editing, motion graphics).

**Examples**:
- Founder VSL (M11 production protocol)
- Professional product shoot (studio lighting, multiple setups)
- Animated explainer video (motion graphics, voiceover)
- Influencer collaboration (paid partnership, full production)

**Production Time**: 1-2 weeks (scripting + coordination + editing + revisions)
**Cost**: $500-2,000+ (M11 VSL production = $800-1,500)
**Hit Rate**: 10-20% (higher production ≠ higher performance, but higher ceiling if it hits)

**When to Use**: Once you've validated a winning angle and want to scale it with premium creative.

[Confidence: B | Source: M11 vsl_as_trade_framework.md, agency benchmarks | Date: 2026-02]

## 3. The Monthly Replenishment Cadence

### 3.1 Month 1-2 (Launch Phase)

**Target Velocity**: 4-6 new ads/week (2-3 Tier 2, 2-3 Tier 1)

**Replenishment Schedule**:
- **Week 1**: Produce 10 Tier 2 ads (5 UGC, 5 founder-led) — bootstrap creative library
- **Week 2**: Test all 10, identify 1-2 winners
- **Week 3**: Produce 5 Tier 1 derivatives of winners + 3 new Tier 2 concepts
- **Week 4**: Continue testing, prepare Month 2 batch

**Budget**: $500-800/month (UGC creators, editing tools)

[Confidence: B | Source: M12 creative_strategy.md velocity targets | Date: 2026-02]

---

### 3.2 Month 3-4 (Validation Phase)

**Target Velocity**: 4-7 new ads/week (1-2 Tier 2, 3-5 Tier 1)

**Replenishment Schedule**:
- **Week 1**: Batch UGC production (5-10 raw videos from 2-3 creators)
- **Week 2**: Edit + test UGC variants, refresh fatigued winners with Tier 1 derivatives
- **Week 3**: Identify new winning hooks, produce Tier 1 swaps
- **Week 4**: Plan Tier 3 asset (VSL or influencer collab) for Month 5

**Budget**: $600-1,200/month (UGC batch + editing + potential influencer seed)

---

### 3.3 Month 5-6 (Scaling Phase)

**Target Velocity**: 7-11 new ads/week (2-3 Tier 2, 5-8 Tier 1)

**Replenishment Schedule**:
- **Week 1**: Launch Tier 3 asset (VSL or premium creative)
- **Week 2**: Test Tier 3 + produce Tier 1 derivatives
- **Week 3**: Batch UGC production (10-15 raw videos, 3-5 creators)
- **Week 4**: Edit UGC, refresh all winners, prepare Month 7 batch

**Budget**: $1,200-2,500/month (Tier 3 production + UGC batch + editing + potential agency support)

---

### 3.4 Month 7-12 (Growth Phase)

**Target Velocity**: 11-17 new ads/week (3-5 Tier 2, 8-12 Tier 1)

**Replenishment Schedule**:
- **Weeks 1-2**: Continuous Tier 1 production (ad refresh every 7-10 days)
- **Week 3**: Batch UGC + influencer content production
- **Week 4**: Test new angles, retire underperformers, plan next month

**Budget**: $2,000-5,000/month (UGC at scale + influencer partnerships + editing + potential agency)

[Confidence: B | Source: M12 creative_strategy.md velocity scaling | Date: 2026-02]

## 4. The Weekly Replenishment Workflow

### 4.1 Monday: Review + Plan

**Tasks**:
1. Review last week's ad performance (M13 daily checklist)
2. Identify fatigued ads (CPA +30%, hook rate -20%, CPM +40%)
3. Check backlog: How many ready-to-test ads remain?
4. Plan this week's production: Tier 1 derivatives? New Tier 2 concepts?

**Decision Rule**:
- Backlog <5 ads = URGENT, produce Tier 1 derivatives immediately
- Backlog 5-10 ads = NORMAL, proceed with planned production
- Backlog >10 ads = HEALTHY, focus on testing current batch

**Time**: 30 minutes

---

### 4.2 Tuesday-Thursday: Produce

**Tier 1 Production** (Tuesday AM):
- Swap hooks on 2-3 winning ads
- Create cut-downs (60s → 15s variants)
- Swap text overlays / CTAs
- Export + upload to Meta/TikTok

**Tier 2 Production** (Tuesday PM - Thursday):
- Script 1-2 new concepts (30 min)
- Film UGC or founder-led video (1-2 hours)
- Edit (2-4 hours)
- Export + upload

**Time**: 4-8 hours/week (Tier 1 + Tier 2 combined)

---

### 4.3 Friday: Launch + QA

**Tasks**:
1. Upload new ads to Meta Ads Manager
2. QA each ad (mobile preview, links work, compliance check)
3. Launch with $20-30 test budget per ad
4. Update creative tracker (M13 ad_swipe_file.md or internal tracker)

**Time**: 1-2 hours

---

### 4.4 Continuous: UGC Batch Production (Monthly)

**Process**:
1. **Recruit 2-5 UGC creators** (Billo, Insense, Trend, Hashtag Paid)
2. **Brief creators**: Send script templates, product, brand guidelines
3. **Receive 5-10 raw videos per creator** (10-30 second clips)
4. **Edit into 15-30s ads** (swap hooks, text overlays, CTAs)
5. **Bank 20-50 UGC variants** for ongoing testing

**Time**: 8-12 hours/month (mostly creator coordination + editing)
**Cost**: $500-1,500/month (creator fees: $50-150/video × 10-15 videos)

[Confidence: B | Source: UGC agency benchmarks (Billo, Insense), M23 influencer_strategy.md | Date: 2026-02]

## 5. Backlog Management System

### 5.1 The Creative Backlog (Google Sheets or Notion)

**Columns**:
- **Ad Name** (e.g., "Missing Mineral Hook A — UGC 15s")
- **Tier** (1, 2, or 3)
- **Angle** (Missing Mineral, Contrarian, Comparison, etc.)
- **Format** (UGC, Founder, Static, etc.)
- **Status** (Ready to Test, In Production, Testing, Retired)
- **Date Added**
- **Test Budget** ($20-50)
- **Performance** (CPA, Hook Rate, ROAS — filled after testing)

### 5.2 Backlog Health Metrics

**Target Inventory**:
- **Tier 1 Ready**: 5+ derivatives (can produce same-day if needed)
- **Tier 2 Ready**: 5+ new concepts (can test immediately)
- **Tier 3 In Production**: 1 asset every 1-2 months

**Health Check** (Weekly):
- Backlog <5 = RED (urgent production needed)
- Backlog 5-10 = YELLOW (monitor closely)
- Backlog 10+ = GREEN (healthy)

[Confidence: A | Source: Creative ops best practices | Date: 2026-02]

## 6. Creative Drought Emergency Protocol

### 6.1 If Backlog Hits Zero

**Immediate Actions** (Day 1):
1. **Pause underperforming ads** (CPA >2x target) to stop bleed
2. **Increase budget on last known winner** (even if fatigued) — buy time
3. **Produce 3-5 Tier 1 derivatives** of last winner (hook swaps, text overlays)
4. **Launch within 24 hours**

**Short-Term** (Days 2-3):
5. **Order rush UGC** from creators (pay premium for 48-hour delivery)
6. **Film founder-led testimonial** (iPhone, natural lighting, 60 seconds)
7. **Repurpose existing content** (cut old winners into new formats)

**Root Cause Fix** (Week 2):
8. **Implement weekly production cadence** (Tier 1 every Tuesday, Tier 2 every Thursday)
9. **Build 10+ ad backlog** before resuming normal operations
10. **Set calendar reminder**: "Creative Backlog Check" every Monday

[Confidence: B | Source: Emergency response patterns from agency experience | Date: 2026-02]

## 7. Replenishment Automation & Tools

### 7.1 Editing Tools for Tier 1 Speed

**Recommended Stack**:
- **CapCut** (free, mobile + desktop): Fast video editing, text overlays, cut-downs
- **Canva Pro** ($13/month): Static ad templates, quick thumbnail swaps
- **Descript** ($12-24/month): AI-powered video editing, transcription, overdub
- **OpusClip** ($9-30/month): AI clip generation from long-form video (auto cut-downs)

**Goal**: Produce Tier 1 derivative in <1 hour.

[Confidence: A | Source: 2024-2025 creator tool ecosystem | Date: 2026-02]

---

### 7.2 UGC Creator Platforms

**Top Platforms**:
1. **Billo** — $150-250/video, 3-5 day turnaround, high quality
2. **Insense** — $100-300/video, 5-7 day turnaround, micro-influencer network
3. **Trend** — $50-150/video, 2-5 day turnaround, fast + affordable
4. **Hashtag Paid** — $150-500/video, influencer + UGC hybrid

**Recommendation**: Start with Trend (affordable, fast), upgrade to Billo once profitable.

[Confidence: A | Source: M23 creator_strategy.md, UGC platform comparisons 2024 | Date: 2026-02]

---

### 7.3 AI Tools for Rapid Iteration (Experimental)

- **ChatGPT/Claude** — Script generation, copy variants

**Use Case**: Generate 10 hook variants in 5 minutes (AI script generation) → human edits best 3 → test.

**Confidence**: C (experimental, not yet proven at scale for supplement D2C ads)

## 8. Replenishment Budgeting

### 8.1 Creative Production Budget by Phase

| Phase | Monthly Creative Budget | Breakdown |
|-------|------------------------|-----------|
| **Month 1-2** | $500-800 | $300 UGC (2-3 creators × $100-150/video), $200 editing tools, $0-300 founder-led (DIY) |
| **Month 3-4** | $600-1,200 | $400 UGC (3-5 creators), $200 editing tools, $0-600 Tier 3 prep (influencer seed) |
| **Month 5-6** | $1,200-2,500 | $500 UGC, $300 editing/tools, $400-1,700 Tier 3 (VSL or influencer collab) |
| **Month 7-12** | $2,000-5,000 | $800-1,500 UGC (5-10 creators), $500 editing/tools, $700-3,000 Tier 3 + influencer partnerships |

**Rule of Thumb**: Creative production budget = 15-25% of total ad spend.

**Example**: $3,000/month ad spend → $450-750/month creative production budget.

[Confidence: B | Source: D2C creative budget benchmarks, agency rule of thumb | Date: 2026-02]

---

### 8.2 ROI Justification

**Creative ROI Math**:
- Spend: $500/month on UGC + editing
- Output: 10 new ad variants/month
- Hit Rate: 1-2 winners (10-20%)
- Winner Impact: 20-40% lower CPA (M13 research)

**Example Scenario**:
- Baseline CPA: $40
- Winner CPA: $28 (30% improvement)
- Monthly orders: 50
- Savings: 50 orders × $12 = $600/month
- Creative Cost: $500/month
- Net Gain: +$100/month + ongoing benefits

**Conclusion**: Creative production pays for itself within 1 month if 1-2 winners emerge.

[Confidence: B | Source: M13 CPA improvement benchmarks from hook testing | Date: 2026-02]
