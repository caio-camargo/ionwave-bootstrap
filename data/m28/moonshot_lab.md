## 1. The Rules

1. No idea is too crazy to write down
2. Don't evaluate while brainstorming
3. Steal shamelessly from other industries
4. Ask "What if we did the opposite?"
5. 90% of these will be bad. That's the point.
6. The goal is ONE idea that's 10x, not ten ideas that are 1.1x

### Activation Lock *(Dialogue Upgrade U7)*

The Moonshot Lab is a **CAPTURE tool now, an EXECUTION tool later.** Without milestone gates, moonshots become founder procrastination.

| D2C MRR | Lab Status | Allowed Activity |
|---------|-----------|-----------------|
| <$50K | **READ-ONLY** | Capture ideas only. No evaluation, no experiments. All focus on core business |
| $50K-$100K | **EVALUATE** | May allocate max 5% of time (2 hrs/week) to scoring and evaluating ideas |
| >$100K | **EXPERIMENT** | May run 1 moonshot experiment per quarter. Budget cap: 5% of monthly revenue |

## 3. Evaluation Framework

**Rule**: Only evaluate AFTER you have 20+ ideas. Don't kill ideas too early.

| Score (1-10) | Criteria | Weight |
|-------------|----------|--------|
| 1-10 | Potential upside if it works | 3x |
| 1-10 | Feasibility with current resources | 2x |
| 1-10 | Alignment with brand/mission | 2x |
| 1-10 | Speed to test (can we learn fast?) | 2x |
| 1-10 | Defensibility (can others copy easily?) | 1x |

**Total Score = Sum of (Score × Weight). Max = 100.**

### Quick Triage (Before Full Scoring)

|----------|-------------------|--------|
| **Test This Week** (Low effort, high learning) | #5 Open source formula, #12 Lifetime supply | Run as experiment |

| **Kill** (Low upside, high effort, or off-brand) | #4 Weather subscription, #6 Sommelier service | Archive |

## 5. Prompts for More Ideas

Use these when brainstorming sessions stall:

1. What if we charged 10x more? What would have to be true?
2. What if we gave it away free? How would we make money?
3. What would [Apple/Tesla/Patagonia] do in our space?
4. What's the most annoying thing about buying supplements?
5. Who else has the same customer but isn't competing with us?
6. What if we only sold to businesses, not consumers?
7. What adjacent problem could we solve?
8. What would make someone tattoo our brand on their body?
9. What if we were a media company that happened to sell electrolytes?
10. What would a non-American market want that we're not offering?
