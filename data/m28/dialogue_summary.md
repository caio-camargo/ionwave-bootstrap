## Key Insights

### From Skeptical Investor
- Amazon cannibalization risk is real — price parity + D2C subscription advantage are critical
- Retail requires $50-200K+ working capital per tier — capital planning must precede retail entry
- B2B unit economics are excellent (LTV:CAC 13.2x) IF retention holds — auto-reorder is the key lever
- Subscription conversion sensitivity: viable down to 30%, but below 20% is a crisis

### From Growth Engineer
- Attribution must be simple at low spend — post-purchase survey + UTM + unique codes
- SEO is Phase 2 (Month 3-6), NOT pre-launch — every pre-launch hour must drive Day 1 revenue
- VOC pipeline is critical for measurable product triggers — without it, SKU expansion is gut-driven
- Product iteration needs quarterly cadence with reformulation gates (60% blind test threshold)

### From Retail Veteran
- MAP pricing is non-negotiable — without it, wholesale accounts undercut D2C
- Amazon cold start requires D2C customer seeding, NOT just PPC
- Gym/B2B accounts churn 60-70% without auto-reorder — default must be auto-ship
- White label creates competitors — guardrails on formula, territory, and volume are essential
- Moonshot lab needs activation lock — below $50K MRR it's a procrastination tool
