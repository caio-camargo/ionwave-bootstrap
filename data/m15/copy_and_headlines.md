## 1. Headline Formula Library

Headlines are ~80% of conversion. A page with a great headline and average body copy will outperform a page with an average headline and great body copy.

### Headline Principles
- **Specific > Vague** — "78 minerals" beats "lots of minerals"
- **Benefit > Feature** — "All-day energy" beats "ionic mineral blend"
- **You > We** — "Your body knows" beats "We harvested"
- **Numbers > Generalities** — "30 seconds" beats "quick"
- **Curiosity + Clarity** — Hook them AND tell them what it is
- **5th-7th grade reading level** — Converts at 11% vs 5% for professional level

### Formula Library (Deduplicated)

| # | Type | Formula | IonWave Example |
|---|------|---------|----------------|
| 1 | **BENEFIT** | [Get Benefit] Without [Pain] | "Get Sustained Energy Without the Caffeine Crash" |
| 2 | **HOW TO** | How To [Achieve Desire] (Even If [Objection]) | "How To End Brain Fog In 3 Days" |
| 3 | **QUESTION** | [Question That Implies Solution]? | "Still Feeling Dehydrated No Matter How Much Water You Drink?" |
| 4 | **NUMBER** | [Number] [Things] For [Benefit] | "78 Minerals Your Body Is Missing" |

| 17 | **SIMPLE** | [Benefit]. [Benefit]. [Benefit]. | "More Energy. Clearer Mind. Better Performance." |

### Subheadline Rules
The subheadline expands on the headline by adding ONE of:
- Specificity/proof ("78 ocean-sourced minerals, zero sugar, 30-day guarantee")
- Secondary benefit ("Plus faster recovery and better sleep")
- Objection reduction ("No pills, no powders, just 30 seconds")
- Urgency ("Limited first-batch pricing")

### Testing Priority
Per M14 creative testing protocol:
1. **Test headline type first** (benefit vs. question vs. social proof) — biggest impact
2. **Then test angle** (energy vs. hydration vs. mineral deficiency) — audience resonance
3. **Then test specific wording** (synonyms, numbers, phrasing) — fine-tuning
4. **Winner = highest conversion, not highest clicks** — CTR ≠ CVR

## 2. Page Section Templates

### Hero Section Template
```
[HEADLINE: Primary benefit in 5-10 words]
[SUBHEADLINE: Support headline, add specificity]
[HERO IMAGE: Product shot or lifestyle context]
[CTA BUTTON: Action verb + Offer — e.g., "Try Risk-Free — $47"]
[MICRO-COPY: "30-day guarantee" under button]
[SOCIAL PROOF: "★★★★★ [X] reviews | Free shipping"]
```

### Problem/Agitation Section Template
```
[SECTION HEADLINE: Call out the problem they recognize]

• Symptom 1 they identify with (afternoon energy crashes)
• Symptom 2 they identify with (brain fog and poor focus)
• Symptom 3 they identify with (muscle cramps after workouts)
• Symptom 4 they identify with (drinking water but still dehydrated)

[AGITATION: What happens if they don't solve it]
[TRANSITION: Bridge to solution — "There's a reason..."]
```

### Solution Section Template
```
[BRIDGE: "The problem isn't you. It's your [category]."]
[INTRODUCE PRODUCT: "That's why we created..."]
[KEY DIFFERENTIATOR: Why this is different from everything else]
[MECHANISM: How it works (the "why" behind the product)]
```

### Benefits Section Template (NOT Features)
```
Each benefit:
• [ICON/IMAGE]
• [BENEFIT HEADLINE — what life looks like after]
• [1-2 sentence explanation — why this benefit happens]

Rule: Feature = what it is. Benefit = why they care.
❌ Feature: "78 trace minerals"
✓ Benefit: "Complete cellular hydration — energy, clarity, recovery"
❌ Feature: "Ocean-sourced"
✓ Benefit: "Minerals your body recognizes and absorbs (98% bioavailable)"
```

### Social Proof Section Template
```
[SECTION HEADLINE: "What Our Community Is Saying"]
[TESTIMONIAL 1-3, each with:]
  - Photo (real person) / verified buyer badge
  - Name, role/location
  - Star rating
  - Quote (specific result, not generic praise)
[AGGREGATE: "★★★★★ [X] reviews" or "Join [X] customers"]
```

### Comparison Section Template (Updated with U20)
```
[SECTION HEADLINE: "How [Product] Compares"]
[TABLE or visual comparison grid:]
| Feature | Status Quo (Nothing) | Competitor A | Competitor B | IonWave |
|---------|---------------------|-------------|-------------|---------|
| Minerals | 0 (continued depletion) | 3 | 10-20 | 78 |
| Source | N/A | Synthetic | Synthetic | Ocean-harvested |
| Bioavailability | N/A | Unknown | ~10% (pill) | 98% (ionic) |
| Price | $45/mo | $20-40/mo | $47/mo |
```

### FAQ Section Template
```
Must-have questions (in order of importance):
1. What makes this different from [competitor]?
2. How long until I feel results?
3. What does it taste like?
4. Is it safe? Can I take with other supplements?
5. What if I don't like it? (→ guarantee)
6. How does shipping/subscription work?
7. Where do you source it?
8. Is it safe during pregnancy? (→ "consult your doctor")

Each answer should: reduce anxiety AND increase desire.
```

## 4. IonWave Product Page Copy (PDP Reference Draft)

The PDP is the page most visitors will land on from retargeting, brand search, and warm traffic. It must convert without the education that a landing page provides.

### PDP Structure
1. Product title + subtitle
2. Pricing display (subscription pre-selected per M9/M10)
3. Star rating + review count
4. Short description (above fold) — 3-4 lines + bullet points
5. How to use (3 steps)
6. What's inside (ingredient breakdown)
7. Benefits section (5 benefits)
8. Social proof / reviews
9. FAQ section (8 questions)
10. Trust badges
11. Add to cart (subscription vs one-time toggle)

### Key PDP Copy Decisions
- **"No BS" positioning** runs through all copy — direct, confident, no marketing fluff
- **Per-serving cost framing**: $1.57/day (subscription) makes $47/month feel small
- **Competitor comparison** is implicit on PDP (explicit on LP) — "Unlike synthetic electrolytes..."
- **Subscription is always the recommended option** — visually prominent, pre-selected

## 5. Copy Testing Protocol

Aligned with M14 testing framework:

| Priority | Test | Method | Sample Size | Decision |
|----------|------|--------|-------------|----------|
| P0 | Headline type (benefit vs question vs social proof) | A/B on LP | 500+ clicks per variant | Highest CVR wins |
| P1 | Hero CTA copy ("Try Risk-Free" vs "Shop Now" vs "Get Started") | A/B on LP + PDP | 300+ clicks per variant | Highest ATC rate |
| P2 | Social proof placement (below hero vs after problem section) | A/B on LP | 500+ clicks | Highest scroll depth + CVR |
| P3 | FAQ content (6 questions vs 8 vs 10) | A/B on PDP | 300+ clicks | Highest CVR, monitor time-on-page |
| P4 | Long-form vs short-form LP | Architecture test | $500+ per variant | Per M15 funnel testing protocol |
