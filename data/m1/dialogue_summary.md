## Key Themes

1. **Legal risk is the #1 gap** — Contractor misclassification (U4), equity instrument unspecified (U23), Trustee authority without legal backing (U15)
2. **CM independence is structural** — Cannot report through CoS who has equity incentives (U18)
3. **Offboarding is as important as onboarding** — No transition protocol existed (U32)
4. **Assessment and verification are weak** — Onboarding has no gates (U3), certification has no rubric (U20)
5. **Equity needs specificity** — Type, valuation, pivot/dissolution treatment, drag/tag rights all unspecified
6. **Budget is understated** — Labor-only budget misses tools, legal, insurance, ad spend (U12)
7. **Franchise parallel validates the model** — 10% failure rate with documented systems vs 60% without
8. **Double-trigger is modern standard** — Single-trigger acceleration is outdated (U5)
