### 1.2 Budget at Conservative Margins (42% GM fully-loaded)

At Conservative margins, COGS increases by ~$35K annually (higher fulfillment from 3PL, higher returns). Net Y1 loss expands from -$99K to ~-$135K.

### 1.3 Budget at Optimistic Margins (67% product GM only)

If measuring product GM only (Bootstrap method), the P&L shows product COGS of ~$113K on $490K revenue = 77% product GM. This is misleading for budgeting but valid for tracking product cost efficiency.
